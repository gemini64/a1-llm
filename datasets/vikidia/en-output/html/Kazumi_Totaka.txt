Kazumi Totaka (born August 23, 1967) is a Japanese composer and sound director for video games who is mainly known for his compositions in Nintendo games. He sometimes does dubbing.


Dubbing
He mainly voices Yoshi in Mario but also Professor E. Gadd (Luigi's Mansion), Captain Olimar (Pikmin), Birdo (Mario) and Kéké Laglisse (Animal Crossing)
