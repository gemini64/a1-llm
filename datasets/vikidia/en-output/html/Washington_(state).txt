Washington is a state in the northwestern United States. In 2015, it had 7,170,351 people. (*)
Olympia is the state capital, and Seattle is the largest city, Spokane being the 2nd largerst.
Washington is bordered by the Canadian province British Columbia to the north, Idaho to the east, Oregon to the south, and the Pacific Ocean to the west.
