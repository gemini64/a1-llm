LeBron James is a professional basketball player in the NBA. He was born on December 30, 1984 in Akron, Ohio. He plays both forward and power forward positions. James has played for the Cleveland Cavaliers and the Miami Heat. He currently plays for the Los Angeles Lakers. He is a 12 time All-Star, 4th time best player in a season, and has he has 3 NBA titles (with 3 final MVP nominations), not to mention his multiple Olympic titles won with the United States.
