Neum
Neum is a small and the only town that has the sea in Bosnia and Herzegovina. The city has a pebble beach and about 20 kilometers of coastline. The city is not an open sea because it has a peninsula in front of it. The city has the best sewage system on the Adriatic Sea unlike cities in other countries. The city has hotels while the best is the Grand Hotel Neum with swimming pools, and it is also the largest hotel in Neum with four stars.
