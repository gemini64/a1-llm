Chess board with pieces: in the center of each row, we can see the Queen and the King, with their crowns.
Chess is a board game. It is one of the oldest board games still played, originating in the 6th century. Chess is considered to be a difficult game and requires a lot of strategy.


Players

Chess games are played by two players.

Chess pieces

Queen with a crown
King with a crown too
Bishop with his hat
Knight with his horse
Rook or Tower
Pawns, simple soldiers
