The San Jose Sharks are a National Hockey League team from San Jose, California.  They play in the Pacific Divsion, of the Western Conference. The San Jose Sharks (In Blue) playing the Anaheim Ducks

History
The San Jose Sharks began playing in the NHL in 1991 Season.

Stanley Cup
In the 2016 Playoffs, the San Jose Sharks went to the Stanley Cup after beating the St Louis Blues, but lost the Stanley Cup to the Pittsburgh Penguins.
