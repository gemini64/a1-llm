Cape Town is the legislative capital of South Africa. It is one of the three capitals of the country, in addition to Pretoria (administrative) and Bloemfontein (judicial).
Cape Town is also the second most populous city in South Africa, after Johannesburg. As of 2009, the urban area had 3.353 million people. (*)
