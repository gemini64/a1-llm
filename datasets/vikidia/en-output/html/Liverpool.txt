A few famous sights in Liverpool
Liverpool is a city in England, United Kingdom. Liverpool has a population of 491,500, but in its metropolitan area 2.24 million people live.
Liverpool has a huge port, where the RMS Titanic was built. Liverpool is noted for its culture, architecture and links in transport. It has after London the most art galleries, national museums and monuments.
Many famous people lived of were born in Liverpool. The Premier League football clubs Liverpool F.C. and Everton F.C. are both based in Liverpool.
