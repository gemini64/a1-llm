Un p'tit truc en plus is a French film directed by Artus and released in 2024.

Plot
After robbing a jewelry store, a father and his son will have to hide in a summer camp for people with disabilities.

Production


Development
Filming began on July 17, 2023 in Malleval-en-Vercors and ends on September 2, 2023 in Saint-Nazaire-en-Royans.

Release
The film's preview took place at the Rencontres du cinéma de Gérardmer on April 10, 2024. Un p'tit truc en plus was released on May 1, 2024.
