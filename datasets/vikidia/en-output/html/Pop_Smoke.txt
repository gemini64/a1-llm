Pop Smoke, (real name Bashar Barakah Jackson), was a rapper. He was born on July 20, 1999, in Brooklyn, New York. In February 2020, Pop Smoke was murdered during a home invasion in his friend's home in Los Angeles. He mainly did a style of music called Brooklyn Drill and released two mixtapes before his death and two studio albums after.
50 Cent executive produced Pop Smoke's first studio album Shoot for the Stars, Aim for the Moon.
