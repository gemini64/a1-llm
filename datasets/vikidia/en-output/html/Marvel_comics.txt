Marvel comics is a comic company based in America, previously known as atlas comics and timely comics. It was as timely comics that it was known when it was created in 1939. Famous writers and artists of Marvel comics fame are Stan Lee, Chris Claremont, Tom deFalco, John Romita Sr, John Romita Jr, and more. The fantastic four are the superhero team credited with kicking off the Marvel comics era, and other popular teams include the Avengers, X-men, West Coast Avengers, and other well known and loved superhero teams, featuring characters such as Wolverine, Iron Man, Captain America, the Hulk, Doctor Strange, and more!
