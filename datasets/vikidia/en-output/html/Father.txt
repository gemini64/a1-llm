A father is the male biological parent of a child.
A father can also be called "dad", "dada", "daddy", "papa", "pappy", "pop", "pa", "da", "fader", "fater", "poppy", and "poppa".
Seahorses are the only animals in which the father gives birth instead of the mother.
A stepfather is a man married to the child's mother after the divorce or death of the father.
