Bar Luce at Largo Isarco, near Via Ripamonti
Bar Luce is a restaurant located at Largo Isarco, 2, Milan, Italy, owned by Prada.

History
Bar Luce was opened in 2015 by Miuccia Prada.
The Bar Luce was designed in 2015 by the director Wes Anderson, who wanted to recreate a "Milanese" bar from the 1950s, the considered period of the birth of Italian design and then appreciated all over the world.

Patrons
Bar Luce made by Prada, has long been frequented by famous people and vip of art and fashion system.
