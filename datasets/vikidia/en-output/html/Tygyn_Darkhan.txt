Tygyn Darkhan (Yakut: Тыгын Дархан; 1580(?)–1632), was a legendary figure in Yakut folklore and history. He was a chief of the Kangalas ulus (clan) and considered the king of the Yakuts, an indigenous Turkic people living in the Sakha Republic (Yakutia) of Russia.
