Luiz de Orléans e Bragança (6 June 1938 – 15 July 2022) was the head of the Vassouras branch of the House of Orléans-Braganza from 1981 until his death 2022.
He held the titles of Prince of Orleans-Braganza from 1938 to 1981, Imperial Prince of Brazil from 1938-1981 and was the head of the Imperial House of Brazil, from July 5, 1981 until his death
Luiz died on 15 July 2022 at a hospital in São Paulo, Brazil from stroke-related problems, aged 84.
