Information is facts or knowledge learned about a certain event or subject. (*)  information is the act of telling others news about something. A newspaper, for example, contains information about events that have happened. This article contains information about ``Information``. Information is usually described as one or more statements or facts that are received by a human and that have some kind of importance to the person receiving the information. (*)  Information generally has four qualities:

That it has to be something.
That it be new information.
That it be true. False information is called "misinformation".
In addition to being something itself, it be about something.
