Doraemon
Doraemon is a fictional character created by Fujiko Fujio that is part of the Doraemon series.

Creation


Features
Doraemon (born September 3, 2112, Virgo) is an earless blue robot cat. It was built by the Matsusibu Company in the twentieth century. His original name was MS 903. A part of Doraemon was accidentally lost during construction. So he is different from other robots. He is messy and can't do any work properly. He has a four-dimensional pocket with gadgets. She moved to the past to improve the life of her owner Shivasi, her great grandfather Nobita. He is Nobita's closest friend. They quarrel occasionally. Doraemon is always worried about Nobita. Once a robot rat ate his ears. So he is afraid of rats. Doracake is his favorite food. He is annoyed with his sister Dorami. But she loves him.
