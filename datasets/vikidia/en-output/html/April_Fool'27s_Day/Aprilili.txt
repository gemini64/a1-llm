Aprilili is a colloquial name for the Day of the April Fool's Day joke, which is named after the phrase apri-li-li-li used at the end of the joke. According to the old custom, every year on April 1, people joke with each other and cheat each other with harmless tricks, and after they trick someone, they announce that they have been tricked into April.
