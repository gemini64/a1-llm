Derai is an upazila of the Sunamganj District in northeastern Bangladesh, part of the Sylhet Division. (*)

Population
Total 243690; male 122636, female 121054; Muslim 179222, Hindu 64285, Buddhist 11, Christian 78 and others 94. (*)
