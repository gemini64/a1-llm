Location of the Czech Republic in Europe
The Czech Republic, (*)  (Czech language:Česká republika)  (*)  also known by its short-form name Czechia (*)  and historically known as Bohemia, (*)  is a country in central Europe. Its capital and main city is Prague. The Czech Republic has been a member of the European Union since 2004.
