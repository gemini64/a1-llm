Godinne is a village in Belgium in the province of Namur in the comune of Yvoir. The river Meuse passes along it.

What is there in Godinne

A library,opened nearly all week expect Sunday and Monday
Serval restaurants,the most famous the other side of the Meuse up the hill. It's called Les 7 Meuses (The 7 Meuse in french)
Some shops, the biggest one is Spar
A train station with trains going twoards Namur and Brussels and to Dinant and Libramont the other way
Two schools : L'ecole comunale is a nursery and primary school.Le College Saint-Paul with a little part that is primary,the biggest part is for secondary
Two playgrounds
A tennis and football terain,but there are also varios terains belonging to the Grand College
