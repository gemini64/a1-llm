Kirby's Dreamland is a videogame


gameplay
The game is black and white. Kirby himself can walk or if you press the > button twice he can run. Kirby can also do more than just walk. He can also fly or suck in enemies. When you are in the water, he can swim.

plot

The plot is very simple: the story is about a penguin who steals food. That penguin is King Dedede, so Kirby goes to Dreamland (the name of the thorp where Kirby walks).
