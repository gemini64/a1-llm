PlayStation 4
The PlayStation 4 is a gaming console which was created by Sony Interactive Entertainment. It is a home video game console developed by Sony Interactive Entertainment. Announced as the successor to the PlayStation 3 in February 2013, it was launched on November 15, 2013, in North America, November 29, 2013, in Europe, South America, and Australia, and on February 22, 2014, in Japan. at similar times to the other eighth-generation consoles, the Wii U and the Xbox One. The PlayStation 4 has sold over 40 million copies worldwide.
