Ontario is a province in southern Canada. It is the most populous province in Canada. In 2014, it had 13,620,646 people. (*)
Toronto is the provincial capital and largest city.
Ontario is bordered by the Hudson Bay to the north, Quebec to the east, the Great Lakes and the United States to the south, and Manitoba to the west.
