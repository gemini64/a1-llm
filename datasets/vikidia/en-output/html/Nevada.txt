Nevada is a state in the western United States. In 2015, it had 2,890,845 people. (*)
Carson City is the state capital, and Las Vegas is the largest city.
Nevada is bordered by Oregon to the northwest, Idaho to the northeast, Utah to the east, Arizona to the southeast, and California to the west.
