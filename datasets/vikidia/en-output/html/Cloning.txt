Cloning of a sheep

From a sheep extracted ovum;
Ovule takes off the nucleus with DNA;
It fetches a cell from the mammary gland of another sheep, different from the first;
Since this cell is extracted the nucleus, whose DNA contains the entire genetic heritage;
The core of mammary cell is entered into the ovule;
The egg is implanted in the uterus of a host sheep and it develops the embryo;
After pregnancy a sheep born with a genetic identical to that of the donor sheep of the cell of the mammary gland;
