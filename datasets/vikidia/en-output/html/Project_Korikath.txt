Project Korikath is a project that working to bridge the knowledge gap in the open knowledge ecosystem of the Wikimedia movement in various formats.


This project works with visual knowledge by donating images in Wikimedia Commons and utilising those in various projects of wikimedia. Under this project, the members identify places and monuments in their locality with Wikipedia entries that don't have quality images and do photowalks personally to provide those Wikipedia entries with images.

History
It was founded on August 16, 2022 in Bangladesh by Wasiul Bahar. (*)
