The Quadronno bag is a Canvas handbag designed by the Milano-based, high-fashion luxury-goods manufacturer Bottega Veneta. The bag is now an expensive status symbol.

History
In 2020 Matthieu Blazy designed a simple and plain bag inspired by the Milanese luxury Quadronno district, frequented by fashion designers such as Miuccia Prada and Gianni Versace where Milan's high aristocracy resides.
