The afterlife is a place or state in many religions and folklore where souls go after death.

Examples

Heaven (Christianity)
Hell (*)  (Christianity)
Purgatory (some Christian denominations)
Limbo
Underworld
Jannah (Islam)
Jahannam (Islam)
Hades (Greek mythology)
Svarga (Hinduism)
Sovngarde (Norse mythology)
