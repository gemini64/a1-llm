May. Corn fields in Germany.
May is the fifth month of the year according to the Gregorian Calendar. The month has 31 days.
In the northern hemisphere May is a month of spring.
In the southern hemisphere May is a month of autumn.
The astrological signs for May are Taurus (April 21 to May 21) and Gemini (May 22 to June 21).

Events in May

1 May : Labour Day or May Day
5 May : Cinco de Mayo (Fifth of May)
9 May : Europe Day, anniversary of the Declaration of Robert Schuman (1950) that lead to the creation of European communities, which in turn eventually evolved into the European Union.
