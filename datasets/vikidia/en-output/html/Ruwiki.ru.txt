Ruwiki is a Russian language online encyclopedia from Russia. It was started in July 2023 as a fork of the Russian Wikipedia. (*)  It was created by Vladimir Medeyko, who was previously involved with the Russian Wikipedia project. (*)   The full-scale launch of Ruwiki.ru took place on January 15, 2024.
