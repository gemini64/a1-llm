Boishakh is the first month of the Bangabd or Bengali calendar and the second month of the Shakabd or Indian Rashtriya Panchang. It is the first month of the Nepali calendar, Vikram Sambat, and Punjabi Nanakshahi calendar. The month of Baisakh is the last half of April and the first half of May in the Gregorian calendar. This month is called Madhava month in the Vedic calendar and Madhusudan month in the Vaishnava calendar.
