Groudon is a legendary Pokémon. He's the iconic Pokemon of two Pokémon games:Pokémon Ruby and Pokemon Ruby Omega. His rival is Kyogre.


Etymology
Groudon's name could come from ground and don,dinosaur in japanese.

Appearance

Groudon look like a ankylosaurus with ancient drawings on his knees.He has spines on his flanks and blades on his tail. Groudon is biped.
