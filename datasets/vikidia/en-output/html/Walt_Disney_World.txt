Walt Disney World's main logo.
Walt Disney World is a theme park and resort in Orlando, Florida, that is divided into four parks: which are The Magic Kingdom, Epcot, Hollywood Studios and The Animal Kingdoms. It includes the Monorail, which goes to Disney's Contemporary Resort, Transportation and Ticket Center, (connecting route to Epcot), Disney's Polynesian Village Resort, Disney's Grand Floridian Resort & Spa, and the Magic Kingdom. It is the largest theme park in the world. (*)
