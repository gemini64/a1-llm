The Eurasian woodcock (scientific name : Scolopax rusticola) is a bird of the Scolopacidae family. This woodcock lives in Asia, Europe and North Africa.

Description
The adult bird is about 35 cm (15 inches).

Behaviour

Feeding
The Eurasian woodcock eats earthworms, spiders, molluscs and insects.
