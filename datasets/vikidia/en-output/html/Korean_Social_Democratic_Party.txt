The Korean Social Democratic Party (KSDP) is a Social Democratic party in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. It was founded on the 3rd of November, 1945, as the Korean Democratic Party. It won 38 candidates in the 1948 elections, and it did manage to win a seat in South Korea as well. It won 50 seats in 2014, and it is assumed to have won 50 seats in 2019.
