Rickshaw in Dhaka
Rickshaw is a human-powered vehicle that has been a popular mode of transportation in many parts of the world for centuries. It is a type of light, two or three-wheeled cart designed to be pulled or pedalled by a person, usually a human driver known as a rickshaw puller. Rickshaws come in various forms and have played a significant role in the urban transportation landscape, providing an affordable and accessible means of travel.
