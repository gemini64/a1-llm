Bluey is an Australian 2018 preschool animated TV series from Queensland production group Ludo Studio. The show was created by Joe Brumm. The series premiered on ABC Kids on October 1, 2018.
This is about a Blue Heeler Puppy named Bluey who is 6 years old (in human years) and lives with her mother (Chilli) father (Bandit) and younger 4-year-old sister, Bingo. The show grew so successful it began to air worldwide. Bluey was and still is a hit cartoon not just in Australia but in America and many other countries as well.
