The Labour Party is a political party in the United Kingdom. It's a centre-left party led by Keir Starmer. The Labour Party is traditionally one of the big political parties in the UK; the other party is the Conservative Party.
The party is currently in power after they won a landslipe in the 2024 general election.

History
The Labour Party was established in 1900 and has a social democratic background. The party governed between 1997 and 2010 under Tony Blair and then Gordon Brown. The Labour Party is a pro-European party. This means the party wants the UK to remain a member of the European Union.
