Rwanda is a country in Africa. It borders Uganda, Tanzania, Burundi and the Democratic Republic of the Congo. Rwanda has 14,426,720 population and it's population density is 571 people per km². The median age in Rwanda is 19.4 years.
(U Rwanda ni igihugu cya Afurika. ruhana imbibi na Uganda, Tanzaniya, Uburundi na Repubulika Iharanira Demokarasi ya Kongo. U Rwanda rufite abaturage 14.426.720 kandi  ubucucike bwabaturage ni 571 kuri km². Ikigereranyo cy' imyaka mu Rwanda ni imyaka 19.4.)
