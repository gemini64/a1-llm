Pennywise is a character name in literature. It may refer to:

In Stephen King's novel It, an evil creature sometimes appears in a form called Pennywise the Dancing Clown.



In Charles King's novel A Trooper Galahad, he is Major Pennywise, a US Army paymaster.



In Pennywise a comic play in Punch (magazine), volume 73, 1877, Pennywise Turniptop is a young wife.



In W. H. M. Holmes's novel Oakleigh, or, The Minor of Great Expectations, Mark Pennywise is a trusty clerk.
