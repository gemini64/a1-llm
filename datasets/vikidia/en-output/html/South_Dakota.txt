South Dakota is a state in the midwestern United States. In 2015, it had 858,469 people. (*)
Pierre is the state capital, and Sioux Falls is the largest city.
South Dakota is bordered by North Dakota to the north, Minnesota to the east, Iowa to the southeast, Nebraska to the south, Wyoming to the west, and Montana to the northwest.
