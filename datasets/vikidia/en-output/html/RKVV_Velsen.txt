RKVV Velsen is a Dutch amateur football club from Driehuis, municipality of Velsen. They play every Sunday in the First Class (2016/17). The club was founded in 1922 as VV Santpoort, but the name was later changed to RKVV Velsen. RKVV Velsen has moved several times from sports park and is currently playing at Sportpark Driehuis, at the Wolff and Dekenlaan near the Dutch railway station Driehuis.
