January is named for the two-faced Roman god Janus
January is the first month of the year according to the Gregorian Calendar. The month has 31 days.
In the northern hemisphere January is a month of winter. Winter begins December 22. In the southern hemisphere January is a month of summer. Summer begins December 22.
