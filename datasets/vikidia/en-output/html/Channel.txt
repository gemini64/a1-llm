Channel, in the English language, can have one of many definitions, including:

A gap between two landmasses wider than a strait, such as the English Channel
A broadcast feed, such as a television channel or YouTube channel
To direct, as to a resource (*)



Television
In television, a channel refers to a specific broadcast band often centered around something such as news, sports, or a type of entertainment such as comedies.

(*) The American College Student's Dictionary.
