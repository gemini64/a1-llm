50 Cent (real name Curtis James Jackson), also known as Fifty Cent, is an American rapper, actor, television producer, and businessman. He was born on July 6, 1975 in New York, United States.  50 Cent's career began in the late 1990's. His first album on a major record label, Get Rich or Die Tryin' was released in 2003.
50 Cent is also an actor in movies and television who usually is credited under his real name along with his stage name (for example: Curtis "50 Cent" Jackson).
