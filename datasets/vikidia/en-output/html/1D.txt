1D or 1 dimensional space is space in 1 dimension.

Shapes
Its not possible to have a shape going on forever.

Ray
Ray
A Ray is a shape with 1 arrow head and 1 end point. It goes on forever in 1 way.

Line
A Line is a shape. It has length and it goes on forever 2 ways.

Line Segments
A Line segment is a line that ends both ways. They are used to connect shapes.

Perpendicular Line
Made of 2 line segments that intersect at 90 degree angles.

Intersecting Line
Made of 2 line segments that do not intersect at 90 degrees.
