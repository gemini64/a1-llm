----Don't brag about not finding science, don't brag about not finding a place. Do not rejoice with passion To play and laugh in vain ... Stay away from five things, Be in a hurry for five things, If you want to be a man, Your desire, in front of your life, If you grieve for him, Gossip, lies, arrogance, Laziness, vain talk If you know the five enemies. Demand, work, deep thought, Satisfaction, compassion Think of five noble deeds...

Abai Qunanbaiuly (Kazakh: Абай Құнанбайұлы; born 10 August 1845 - 6 July 1904) was a Kazakh poet.

Biography
Abai Qunanbaiuly was born on 10 August 1845, in Abay District, East Kazakhstan, Russian Empire. On 6 July 1904, Abai was died in Abay District, East Kazakhstan, Russian Empire.
