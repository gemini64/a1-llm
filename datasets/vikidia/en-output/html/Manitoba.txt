Manitoba is a province in south central Canada. In 2014, it had 1,275,212 people living in it. (*)
Winnipeg is the provincial capital and largest city in the province.
Manitoba is bordered by Nunavut to the north, the Hudson Bay to the northeast, Ontario to the east, and the U.S. states Minnesota and North Dakota to the south, Saskatchewan to the west, and Northwest Territories to the northwest.
