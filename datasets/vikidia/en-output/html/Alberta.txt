Alberta is a province in southwestern Canada. In 2014, it had 4,111,509 people. (*)
Edmonton is the provincial capital, Calgary is the largest city.
Alberta is bordered by Northwest Territories to the north, Saskatchewan to the east, the U.S. state Montana to the south, and British Columbia to the west.
