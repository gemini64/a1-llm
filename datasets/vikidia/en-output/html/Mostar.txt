Mostar is a city in Bosnia and Herzegovina.
It is one of the most popular cities to visit in Bosnia and Herzegovina, most of which is due to the famous Stari Most (Serbian: "Old Bridge") bridge. The bridge and the old city whose two sides it joins are a UNESCO World Heritage Site. (*)
It is located on the banks of the Neretva River and is the cultural and economic center of Herzegovina, and also the largest city in Herzegovina.
