Bashar al-Assad (born 11 September 1965) was the president of Syria from 2000 until he was overthrown in December 2024. He was also the leader of the Syrian military and the Ba'ath Party. He was the son of Hafez al-Assad, who was president from 1971 until his death in 2000.
