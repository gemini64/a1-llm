Benjamin Netanyahu in 2012
Benjamin "Bibi" Netanyahu (21 October 1949, Tel Aviv) is an Israeli politician who heads the rightwing Likud party, serving as Prime Minister of Israel since 2022, having previously been prime minister in 1996–1999 and 2009–2021.
Netanyahu is the first Israeli prime minister to be born in Israel after the establishment of the State, his predecessors were immigrants. He was raised in a secular Jewish family.
