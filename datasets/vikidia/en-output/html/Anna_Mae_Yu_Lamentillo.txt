Commodore Lamentillo in 2023
Anna Mae Yu Lamentillo (born February 7, 1991) (*)  is a Filipino government official, academic, lawyer, columnist and author, with a track record of civil service spanning eight years and four Presidential administrations. Currently, she is the Undersecretary for Public Affairs and Foreign Relations in the Department of Information and Communications Technology. (*)  (*)  Prior to this, she was Assistant Secretary for Public Affairs of the same department. (*)
