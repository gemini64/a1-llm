Pterophyllum is a small genus of freshwater fish from the family Cichlidae known to most aquarists as angelfish.

Species
The currently recognized species in this genus are: (*)

Pterophyllum altum (Pellegrin, 1903)
Pterophyllum leopoldi (J. P. Gosse, 1963)
Pterophyllum scalare (Ferd Schultze, 1823) (freshwater angelfish)
