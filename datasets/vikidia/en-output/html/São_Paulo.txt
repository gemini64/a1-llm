São Paulo
São Paulo is the largest city in Brazil. It is located in the south along the coast of the Atlantic Ocean. In 2013, the city proper had 11,821,873 people; (*)  and in 2010, the urban area had 19,683,975 people. (*)  Both the city proper and urban area of São Paulo are the largest in Brazil.
São Paulo was one of the host cities of the 2014 FIFA World Cup.
