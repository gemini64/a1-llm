James David Vance (born James Donald Bowman; 2 August 1984) is an American politician, author and Marine veteran who is the Vice-President of the United States since 2025. He is a member of the Republican Party and has served as the United States senator from Ohio since 2023.
He was the running mate of Donald Trump in the 2024 United States presidential election, running against Democrat Kamala Harris and her running mate Tim Walz. The election was won by Trump and Vance.
