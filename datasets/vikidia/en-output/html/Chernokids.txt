Chernokids is a short French CGI-animated science fiction movie that was made in 2010 by five students from the film school Supinfocom. The movie was done in 3-D. (*)
Animations used in the movie were created by Matthieu Bernadat, Marion Petegnief, Nils Boussuge, Florence Ciuccoli, and Clement Deltour.
