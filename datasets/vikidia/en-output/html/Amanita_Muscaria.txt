A Amanita muscaria
Amanita muscaria is a type of fungi. This mushroom is red with white spots. It has a ring and volva.
Its common name is the "Fly Agaric".  It is wholly inedible and highly toxic.  Accidentally ingesting (eating) causes dizziness, nausea, vomiting and damages the liver.  In most cases, ingestion leads to severe illness or even death.
