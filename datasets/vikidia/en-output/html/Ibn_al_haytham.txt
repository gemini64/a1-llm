Hassan Ibn Al Haytham, born on 965 AC or 354 AH, in Basra (Iraq), and died on 1040 AC or 430 AH, is a mathematician, astronomer and physicist of the islamic Golden Age . He was the one who created the projector that inspired the one who created the camera . The projector was put in a little dark room and projected the image from the outside to a piece of cloth in the inside .
