An aunt is the sister of one's parent.
The male equivalent is an uncle.
The aunt of one's spouse is called an aunt-in-law.
The sister of one's father is known as a "paternal aunt" while the sister of one's mother is known as a "maternal aunt".
The sister of one's grandparent is called a great aunt, the sister of one's great grandparent is a great great aunt, the sister of one's great great grandparent is one's great great great aunt and so on and so forth.
