Halo is a sci-fi video game created by Bungie and is owned by Microsoft. It is currently managed by 343 industries. The games are about a war between humanity and an alien race called the Covenant. In most of the games the player is a spartan called the Master Chief.

Halo: Combat Evolved
Halo: Combat Evolved (also known as Halo: CE or Helo 1) was released in 2001. It was the very first of then Halo franchise.

Halo 2
Halo 2 was the second edition of the Halo franchise. It was released in November 2004.  It takes place in many different locations including Earth, a Halo Ring and the Covenant holy city High Charity .
Halo 2 anniversary will be released in November 2014 to mark 10 years since the original Halo 2 was released.

Halo 3
Halo 3 is the third instalment of the Halo franchise. It was released in September 2007. It is set on Planet Earth and on the ark (a Forerunner structure where it is possible to activate all the halo rings.)

Halo Wars
Halo Wars is a strategy game made by Ensemble Studios.. It was released in 2009.

Halo 3 ODST
Halo 3 ODST was released in 2009. It takes place during the events of Halo 2 in the city of New Mombasa.

Halo: Reach
Halo: Reach was released in September 2010. It took place just before the events of Halo: Combat Evolved on planet Reach. It was the last halo game to be created by Bungie.

Halo: Combat Evolved Anniversary
Halo: Combat evolved Anniversary was a remake of Halo: Combat evolved. It was released in 2011, 10 years after the original Halo: Combat evolved was released.

Halo 4
Halo 4 was released in November 2012. It was the first Halo to be managed and created by 343 industries. It was set four years after the events of Halo 3 on the Forerunner planet Requiem.
