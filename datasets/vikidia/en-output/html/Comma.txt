Commas are used for half pauses in a sentence. Commas can change what it means, for example:Let's eat kids means to eat kids and Let's eat, kids means to get kids to eat. Commas are commonly found in greetings and after signature. It can be found in a listing sentence. the last comma in the listing sentence, between and and the last word before and is an oxford comma.
