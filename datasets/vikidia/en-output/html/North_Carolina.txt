North Carolina is a state in the eastern United States. In 2015, it had 10,042,802 people. (*)
Raleigh is the state capital, and Charlotte is the largest city.
North Carolina is bordered by Virginia to the north, the Atlantic Ocean to the east, South Carolina to the south, Georgia to the southwest, and Tennessee to the west.
