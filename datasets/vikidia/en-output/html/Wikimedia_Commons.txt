Wikimedia Commons logo
Wikimedia Commons is a wiki hosted by the Wikimedia Foundation. As of February 2018, it has over 40 million images, videos, sounds, and other media files, (*)  which are available for free use (both in cost and in copyright).
Some wikis, such as Wikipedia and Vikidia, allow users to directly embed media from Wikimedia Commons.
