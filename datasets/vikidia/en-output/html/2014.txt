2014 (MMXIV) was a common year starting on Wednesday of the Gregorian calendar. It was preceded by 2013 and followed by 2015.

Events

January 1st - Latvia adopts the euro as its currency.
February - The ebola virus epidemic begins.
February 7th-23rd - The 2014 Winter Olympics are held in Sochi.
March 8th - Malaysia Airlines Flight 370 disappears.
June 12th-July 13th - The 2014 FIFA World Cup is held in Brazil.
July 17th - Malaysia Airlines Flight 17 is shot down over Ukraine.
November 12th - The Philae spacecraft lands on a comet.
December 28th - AirAsia Flight 8501 crashes.
