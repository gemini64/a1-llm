New Mexico is a state in the northeastern United States. In 2015, it had 2,085,109 people. (*)
Santa Fe is the state capital, and Albuquerque is the largest city.
New Mexico is bordered by Colorado to the north, Oklahoma to the northeast, Texas to the east and southeast, the Mexican states Chihuahua and Sonora to the south, Arizona to the west, and Utah to the northwest.
