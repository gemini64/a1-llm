Photograph of William Henry Harrison
William Henry Harrison (1773-1841) was the ninth president of the United States, serving part of one term in 1841. Harrison died after one month in office. This is the shortest amount of time anyone has been president in the US. He was preceded by Martin Van Buren and was succeeded by John Tyler. William Henry Harrison was the grandfather of the 23rd president Benjamin Harrison.
