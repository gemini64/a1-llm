Mario Kart is a video game series made by Nintendo. The games are Super Mario Kart for the Super Nintendo Entertainment system, Mario Kart 64 for the Nintendo 64, Mario Kart Super circuit for the GBA, Mario Kart Double Dash for the Gamecube, Mario Kart DS for the Nintendo DS, Mario Kart Wii for the Wii, Mario Kart 7 for the Nintendo 3DS, Mario Kart 8 for the Wii U, Mario Kart 8 Deluxe for the Nintendo Switch, and Mario Kart Tour.
