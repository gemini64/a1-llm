Bishwo Shahitto Kendro (BSK), translated as the World Literature Center, is a non-profit organization based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Founded in 1978 by Dr. Muhammad Zafar Iqbal and his wife, Yasmeen Haque, BSK promotes literature, education, and cultural exchange nationally and internationally.

Mission:
Bishwo Shahitto Kendro (BSK) primary mission is to foster a love for literature and learning among people of all ages and backgrounds. Through various initiatives and programs, the organization aims to make literature accessible and enjoyable, enriching lives and promoting social harmony.
