A Light Bulb or lamp is a object usually used on ceilings. Light bulbs are used to light houses and buildings and streets. The 3 main types are incadescents, CFL (Compact fluorescent light bulb), and LED (Light emitting diode).

Dimmer switch
Dimmer switches are switches used with light bulbs to dim or brighten the light.
