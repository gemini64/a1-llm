Nebraska is a state in the central United States. As of 2015, it had 1,896,190 people. (*)
Lincoln is the state capital, but Omaha is the largest city.
Nebraska is bordered by South Dakota to the north, Iowa to the east, Missouri to the southeast, Kansas to the south, Colorado to the southwest, and Wyoming to the west.
