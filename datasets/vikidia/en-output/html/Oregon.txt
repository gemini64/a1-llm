Oregon is a state in the northwestern United States. In 2015, it had 4,028,977 people. (*)
The state capital is Salem, and the largest city is Portland.
Oregon is bordered by Washington to the north, Idaho to the east, Nevada and California to the south, and the Pacific Ocean to the west.
