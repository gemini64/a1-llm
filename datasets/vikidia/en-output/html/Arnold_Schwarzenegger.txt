Arnold Schwarzenegger (born 30 July 1947) is an actor. He did the role of TX in the movie Terminator. His birth name is Arnold Alois Schwarzenegger. He is of American and Austrian nationality.

His family and his children:

His family: His father is called Gustav Schwarzenegger and his mother's name is Aurelia Schwarzenegger.
His children are: Katherine Schwarzenegger, Christina Schwarzenegger, Joseph Baena, Patrick Schwarzenegger and Christopher Schwarzenegger.
