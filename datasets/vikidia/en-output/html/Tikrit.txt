Tikrit is a city in Iraq. The city is known as the origin of the family of the late Iraqi dictator, Saddam Hussein whose full name was Saddam Hussein Abd al-Majid al-Tikriti.  Hussein himself was born in the city of Al-Ajwa which is only 8 miles from Tikrit.  At the last census, held in 2012, 160,000 people lived in the city.
