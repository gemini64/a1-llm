Howard Phillips Lovecraft (20 August 1890 – 15 March 1937) was an American writer. (*)  He is best known for creating the Cthulhu mythos, a fictional universe which includes many powerful cosmic deities.



Biography
H. P. Lovecraft was born on 20 August, 1890, in Providence, Rhode Island. His parents were Winfield Scott Lovecraft and Sarah Susan Lovecraft (née).
