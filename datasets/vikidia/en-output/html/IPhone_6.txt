iPhone 6
The iPhone 6 is a smartphone made by Apple Inc. It was first released in 2014 along with the iPhone 6 Plus.
The device originally came with iOS 8.0, and the latest available version is iOS 9.2.
New features compared to the iPhone 5s include a larger display and near field communication (NFC). It was the most popular smartphone at the time.
