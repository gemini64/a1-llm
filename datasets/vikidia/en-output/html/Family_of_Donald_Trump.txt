Donald John Trump (born 1946) was the 45th president of the United States from 2017 to 2021.
He married Ivana Trump (née Zelníčková; 1949–2022) in 1977 and they got divorced in 1990, they had 2 sons and 1 daughter:

Donald Trump Jr. (born 1977)
Ivanka Trump (born 1981)
Eric Trump (born 1984)


He married Marla Maples (born 1963) in 1993 and they got divorced in 1999, they had one daughter: Tiffany Trump (born 1993). He married Melania Trump (née Knauss; born 1970) and had a son called Barron Trump (born 2006).
Donald Trump's father was Fred Trump (1905–1999) and his mother was Mary Anne Trump (née MacLeod; 1912–2000). Donald Trump has 2 brothers and 2 sisters:

Maryanne Trump Barry (née Trump; 1937–2023)
Fred Trump Jr. (1938–1981)
Elizabeth Trump (born 1942)
Robert Trump (1948–2020)
