West Pakistan, officially known as the western wing of Dominion of Pakistan, existed from 1947 until 1970. It included the provinces of Punjab, Sindh, North-West Frontier Province (now Khyber Pakhtunkhwa), and Balochistan, along with several princely states. (*)
