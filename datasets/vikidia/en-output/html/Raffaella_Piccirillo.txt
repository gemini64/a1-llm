Raffaella Maria Piccirillo (born March 3, 1978) in Cremona - Italy, known professionally as Raffaella Piccirillo, is a singer, songwriter, author, and DJ producer.
She participated in the Italian-British documentary film Italy in a Day - Un giorno da italiani (2014) directed by Gabriele Salvatores (supervised by Ridley Scott
) that was part of the Out of Competition section at the 71st Venice International Film Festival.
Raffaella is the number one Singer - Songwriter artist for the State of Cremona, LOM, IT on the ReverbNation web site.
She became known in America and abroad with her music releases and international music collaborations, hosted on many international music magazines.
Her voice is also knowing and mentioned in web radio for her music showcase, hosting international bands and artists from all over the world.
