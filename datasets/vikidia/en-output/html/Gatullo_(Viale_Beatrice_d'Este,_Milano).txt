Bar Gatullo is a restaurant located at Piazzale di Porta Lodovica, 2 corner with Viale Beatrice d'Este, Milan, Italy.

History
Bar Gatullo was opened in 1961. The bar was frequented by many artists including Lucio Fontana and Umberto Boccioni.
Bar Gatullo has long been frequented by famous people and vip of art and fashion system.
