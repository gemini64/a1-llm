Nightclub Felix in Berlin
A nightclub is a kind of club that is open during the night hours. There is usually a bar where the bartender serves beer, wine etc. Disco's and jazzclubs often have dance floors and stages for live music. Other nightclubs specialize in erotic entertainment, such as stripping, hostesses, or prostitutes. Nightclubs are almost always in big cities such as Istanbul, New York, Berlin, London, Paris and so on.
