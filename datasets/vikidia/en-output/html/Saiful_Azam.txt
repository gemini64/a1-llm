Saiful Azam
Saiful Azam (1941-14 June 2020) was a Bangladeshi aviator and politician. He has served as an Air Force pilot in Bangladesh, Pakistan, Jordan and Iraq. He took part in the 1967 Third Arab-Israeli Six-Day War. In the war he was able to shoot down four Israeli planes, a record so far. Prior to 1971, he served in the Pakistan Air Force. After the independence of Bangladesh, he joined the Bangladesh Air Force. For his outstanding contribution as an aviator, the United States Air Force recognized him as one of the 22 living eagles in the world.
