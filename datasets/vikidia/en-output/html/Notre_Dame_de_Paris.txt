Notre Dame de Paris
Notre Dame de Paris (French: "Our Lady of Paris") is a cathedral located in Paris, France. It is located on a natural island called Île de la Cité ("Island of the City").
It is the most famous cathedral in France, admired for its Gothic architecture, which is typically French. It is a popular tourist site.
