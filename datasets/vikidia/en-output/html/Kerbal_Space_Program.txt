The Kerbal Space Program (KSP) is a space flight simulation video game developed and published by Squad for the Microsoft Windows, macOS, Linux, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One platforms. In the game, players run a new space game, consisting of green humanoid alien teams known as "Kerbals". The game features a realistic orbital physics engine, which allows for various real-life orbital maneuvers such as Hohmann transfer orbits and bi-elliptical transfer orbits.
