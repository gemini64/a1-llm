Kinyarwanda is the language used in Rwanda and neighboring countries.
It is the native language of Rwandans, and it has branches like amashi, igikiga, ikirera and others.
(Ikinyarwanda ni ururimi rukoreshwa mu Rwanda no mubihugu bituranye n' uRwanda.
ni ururimi kavukire rw' abanyarwanda, rukagira n' indimishami zirushamikiyeho nka amashi, igikiga, ikirera nizindi.) Kinyarwanda version
