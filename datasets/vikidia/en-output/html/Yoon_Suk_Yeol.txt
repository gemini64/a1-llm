Yoon Suk-yeol (Korean: 윤석열; born 18 December 1960) is a South Korean lawyer and politician. He became the President of South Korea in 2022. Before that, he was the prosecutor general from 2019 to 2021. On 14 December 2024, he was suspended as president after being impeached. The Constitutional Court is deciding if he will return or be removed permanently.
