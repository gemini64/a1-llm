The Sonali Bag, also known as the Golden Bag, Jute Polymer, or Eco-friendly Poly Bag, is a groundbreaking biodegradable bioplastic created in Bangladesh. This innovative material serves as a sustainable substitute for conventional plastic bags, particularly polythene. It reflects Bangladesh's commitment to tackling environmental issues through scientific research and development. The Sonali Bag is a notable example of the country's efforts to promote eco-friendly solutions and reduce the impact of plastic waste on the environment.
