Utah is a state in the western United States. In 2015, it had 2,995,919 people. (*)
Salt Lake City is the state capital and the largest city.
Utah is bordered by Idaho to the north, Wyoming to the northeast, Colorado to the east, New Mexico to the southeast, Arizona to the south, and Nevada to the west.
