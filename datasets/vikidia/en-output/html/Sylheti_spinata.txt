Sylheti spinata, scientific name Sylhetia spinata, is an insect species belonging to the genus Sylhetia and the family Cicadellidae. It was first described by Mathew and Ramakrishnan in 2002. (*)  This insect species falls under the order Hemiptera and the class Insecta, showcasing its place in the intricate web of biodiversity.

Taxonomic Classification

- Kingdom: Animalia
- Phylum: Arthropoda
- Class: Insecta
- Order: Hemiptera
- Family: Cicadellidae
- Genus: Sylhetia
- Species: Sylhetia spinata. (*)

Description and Discovery

Sylhetia spinata was first documented by scientists Mathew and Ramakrishnan in the year 2002. The intricate details of its appearance, habitat, and behavior contribute to the broader understanding of biodiversity within the insect world. (*)
