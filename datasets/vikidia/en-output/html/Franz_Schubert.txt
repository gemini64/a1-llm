Schubert in 1825
Franz Schubert was an Austrian composer. He was born on 31 January 1797. He died on 19 November 1828. He passed his life in Vienna.
His works include the "Unfinished" Symphony, the piano duet "Military March", the "Trout" Quintet, two song cycles The Maid of the Mill and The Winter Journey, and dozens of songs such as "The Erl King", "Serenade", "Ave Maria", and "The Rosebud".
