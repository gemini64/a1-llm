The Euro 2016 is a football championship in 2016, the 15th edition of the UEFA European championship.
It started in France since Friday 10th June 2016.

Stadiums


All the stadiums where the football players plays, are in France
The football stadiums are in Paris, the capital city ; Saint-Denis ; Lens ; Lille ; Strasbourg ; Nancy ; Lyon ; Saint-Etienne ; Bordeaux ; Toulouse ; Nice ; and Marseille.
