Self-portrait by Rembrandt, 1658
Rembrandt Harmenszoon van Rijn was a Dutch painter and artist. He was born in Leiden on 15 July 1606. He died in Amsterdam on 4 October 1669.
He is famous for his many self-portraits, his pictures with  Biblical themes, and his large paintings like The Night Watch and The Syndics of the Clothmaker's Guild.
