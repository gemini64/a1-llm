BFDI is a web series created by Cary and Micheal Huang that is based on Total Drama Island. It is a competition based game where object like contestants play to get a "BFDI". Several contestants have been eliminated in episodes. This web series has 5 seasons (one is still ongoing), that being BFDI, BFDIA, IDFB, BFB, and TPOT. There were several hosts for each season, one of them doesn't even have a host.
