Kobe Bryant (born in 1978 and died in 2020) was a great basketball player. He played for the Los Angeles Lakers for his entire career in the NBA. Bryant was drafted in 1996 by the Charlotte Hornets before being traded to the Lakers. He then retired from basketball in 2016.
On January 26, 2020, he was going to a sports game with his daughter on a helicopter which included 7 other people. The helicopter then crashed and all 9 of the passengers (including Bryant and the pilot) we're injured and killed.
