Pennsylvania is a state in the northeastern United States. As of 2015, it had population of 12,802,503 people. (*)
Harrisburg is the state capital, but the largest city is Philadelphia.
Pennsylvania is bordered by New York to the north, New Jersey to the east, Delaware to the southeast, Maryland to the south, West Virginia to the southwest, Ohio to the west, and Lake Erie to the northwest.
