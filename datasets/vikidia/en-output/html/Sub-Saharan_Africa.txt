Sub-Saharan Africa, Subsahara or Non-Mediterranean Africa is the area and regions of the continent of Africa that lie south of the Sahara. These includes Central Africa, East Africa, Southern Africa, and West Africa.

Religion
Religious demographics in 2020: (*)

Christianity: 62.0%
Islam: 31.4%
Traditional African religions: 3.2%
No religion: 3.0%
Other: 0.4%
