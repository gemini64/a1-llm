Deltarune is a video game created by Toby Fox, the player controls a teen named Kris, who's determined to save the world with Susie, a monster, and ralsei, a prince from the "Dark world". During their quest to seal the "Dark Fountains" to save the world, Some creatures of the dark world will try to stop them. Deltarune is a possible sequel of Undertale.
