Solomon Islands, also known simply as the Solomons, is a country in Melanesia, part of Oceania consisting of 21 major islands: Guadalcanal, Malaita, Makira, Santa Isabel, Choiseul, New Georgia, Kolombangara, Rennell. Vella Lavella, Vangunu, Nendo, Maramasike, Rendova, Shortland, San Jorge, Banie, Ranongga, Pavuvu, Nggela Pile and Nggela Sule, and Tetepare.
