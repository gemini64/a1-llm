Namur (Dutch: Namen) is a city in Belgium. It is the capital of the province of Namur and of Wallonia. The river Meuse passes in Namur. A famous place would be the Citadelle.

Transport

There are buses in Namur by the company Tec
There were trams but they were destroyed in the 1950s and 1960s to let pass the cars and buses
Namur has a train station with eleven platforms
