Scoutpedia is a free wiki Bengali language local wiki hosted by miraheze. It can also be edited by anyone, just like Wikipedia. It was created on 29 August 2023. It is a database of World Scout history and heritage, Scouting activities. It currently has a total of 71 article.

History
Scoutpedia is the online encyclopedia of world scouting. This is a huge collection of information on Scouting, Rovering, Girl Guiding, and youth activities. Scoutpedia provides information, training, and inspiration for all Scouts, Rovers, Girl Guides, and youth.
