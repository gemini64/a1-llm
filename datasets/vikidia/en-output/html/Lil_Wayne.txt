Dwayne Michael Carter Jr, better known as Lil Wayne is a rapper and musician. He was born on September 27, 1982, in New Orleans, Louisiana. He is known for his gritty lyrics and clever rhymes. Lil Wayne has had many hit songs. His albums have sold millions of copies. Lil Wayne has won a number of Grammy Awards for his music. He is also known for his duets with Nicki Minaj, Eminem and Drake.
