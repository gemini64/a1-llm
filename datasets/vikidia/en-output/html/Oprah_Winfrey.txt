Oprah Winfrey is a TV producer, actor, and publisher. She is the first Black American woman billionaire. Her work has helped
women and people of color worldwide. Oprah was born on January 29, 1954, in Mississippi.
She is best known for creating The Oprah Winfrey Show. The won many Emmy Awards and aired for 25 seasons. In 2011, Oprah founded her own TV network called OWN or the Oprah Winfrey Network.
