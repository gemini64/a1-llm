A Vote is a noun. The meaning of Vote when used as a noun is:

"a formal indication of a choice between two or more candidates or courses of action, expressed typically through a ballot or a show of hands or by voice." (*)



When Vote is used as a verb, the meaning of the word is: "give or register a vote."



verb tenses

"3rd person present: votes
past tense: voted
past participle: voted
gerund or present participle: voting" (*)



Alternate meanings

as a noun

"a formal expression of opinion or choice, either positive or negative, made by an individual or body of individuals." (*)
