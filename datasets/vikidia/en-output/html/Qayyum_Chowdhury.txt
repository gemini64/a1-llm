simple:Qayyum Chowdhury
Qayyum Chowdhury was a Bangladeshi artist and painter. (*)  He won Ekushey Padak (1984) and Independence Day Award (2014) by Government of Bangladesh. (*)  (*)

Born
He was born 9 March 1932 in Feni, Bangladesh. (*)

Education
In 1954, He completed his study from Dhaka Art College (now Faculty of Fine Arts, University of Dhaka). (*)
