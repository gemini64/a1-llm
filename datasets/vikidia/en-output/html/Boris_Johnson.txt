Boris Johnson (born June 19, 1964) is a British politician and the former mayor of London. He was the mayor from 2008 to 2016. He is a member of the Conservative Party.
In 2015 he was elected as an MP for Uxbridge and South Ruislip, part of Greater London.
In 2019 following the resignation of Theresa May as Prime Minister, Boris Johnson won a vote among members of the Conservative political party, to become the new leader. By default this also made him Prime Minister.
Boris Johnson resigned on 6 September 2022.
