Mourão on the day he received the Insignia of the Order of Rio Branco.
Antônio Hamilton Martins Mourão (born August 15, 1953), is a Brazilian politician and Brazilian Army General retired.
He was Vice President of Brazil for 4 years, from  January, 1 2019 to January, 1 2023. He held the highest position a soldier could attain in peace times.
