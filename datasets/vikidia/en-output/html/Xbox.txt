The Xbox is a video game console. The first generation Xbox is a console that was released in 2001. It was the first video game console offered by an American company after the Atari Jaguar stopped sales in 1996. It reached over 24 million units sold by May 2006. Microsoft's second console, theXbox 360, was released in 2005 and has sold 86 million units as of October 2021.
