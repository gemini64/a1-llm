Oklahoma is a state in the south central United States. As of 2015, it had 3,911,338 people. (*)
Oklahoma City is the state capital and most populous city. Other large cities include Tulsa.
Oklahoma is bordered by Kansas to the north, Missouri to the northeast, Arkansas to the east, Texas to the south, New Mexico to the west, and Colorado to the northwest.
