Idaho is a state in the northwestern United States. In 2015, it had 1,654,930 people. (*)
Boise is the state capital and largest city.
Idaho is bordered by British Columbia (a province of Canada) to the north, Montana to the northeast, Wyoming to the east, Utah and Nevada to the south, and Oregon and Washington to the west.
