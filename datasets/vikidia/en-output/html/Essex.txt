Essex is a ceremonial county in the East of England, the county town is Chelmsford.
The name comes from the Old English word Ä’astseaxe meaning "East Saxons".

Districts
Essex is made up of 14 districts:

Uttlesford
Braintree
Colchester
Tendring
Harlow
Epping Forest
Chelmsford
Maldon
Brentwood
Basildon
Rochford
Castle Point
Southend-on-Sea
Thurrock
