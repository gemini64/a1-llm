Klingon (Klingon: tlhIngan Hol) is a conlang (constructed language) spoken by an alien race called the Klingons in Star Trek.
It was created by Marc Okrand, James Doohan, and Jon Povill. It is regulated by Marc Okrand.
There are many Star Trek fans who learn the language. In 1996, there were 12 recorded fluent Klingon speakers.
