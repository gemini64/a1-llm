The Green Party of England and Wales (GPEW) is a political party in the United Kingdom, which is only active in England and Wales. The Green Party has a strong opinion towards climate change and the environment. This ideology is called green politics and the party is leftwing.
The Green Party of England and Wales is the only green party which is represented in the House of Commons and the House of Lords. Northern Ireland and Scotland have their own green party; namely the Green Party in Northern Ireland and the Scottish Green Party. The Wales Green Party is semi-autonomous.
