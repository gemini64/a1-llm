The 18th century is the century from the years CE 1701 to CE 1800.

Major events

The 18th century saw the beginning of the Industrial Revolution in Great Britain. (*)
1756 – Beginning of Seven Years' War
1763 – End of Seven Years' War
1776 – American Revolution
1789 – French Revolution



Significant persons

Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart (1756-1791)
