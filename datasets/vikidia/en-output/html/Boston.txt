Downtown Boston
Boston is the state capital and largest city of Massachusetts, United States. In 2013, the city proper had 645,966 people. (*)
The teams in Boston include the Boston Red Sox (baseball) and Boston Celtics (basketball).
Prudential Building and John Hancock Building are two tall buildings in Boston. The Bruins are their hockey team.
