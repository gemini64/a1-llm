The iPod touch 6th generation, the newest model of the iPod touch
The iPod touch is a music and video player made by Apple Inc. It can also be used as a handheld game device, digital camera, and mobile Internet browser.
It is a type of iPod. It runs the iOS operating system, like the iPhone and iPad.
