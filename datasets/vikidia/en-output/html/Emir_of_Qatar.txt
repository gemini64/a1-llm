The emir of the State of Qatar is the monarch and head of state of Qatar. All of the Emirs have been members of the House of Al Thani, whose origins are in the Banu Tamim, one of the largest tribes in the Arabian Peninsula.
The current Emir is Tamim bin Hamad Al Thani, his heir presumptive is Abdullah bin Hamad Al Thani.

List of rulers

Sheikh Ali bin Abdullah Al Thani (1949–1960)
Sheikh Ahmad bin Ali Al Thani (1960–1972 (*) )
Sheikh Khalifa bin Hamad Al Thani (1972–1995)
Sheikh Hamad bin Khalifa Al Thani (1995–2013)
Sheikh Tamim bin Hamad Al Thani (2013–present)
