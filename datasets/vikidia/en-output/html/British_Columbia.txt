British Columbia is a province in southwestern Canada. In 2014, it had 4,624,321 people. (*)
Victoria is the provincial capital, and Vancouver is the largest city.
British Columbia is bordered by Yukon to the north, Northwest Territories to the northeast, Alberta to the east, the U.S. states Washington, Idaho, and Montana to the south, the Pacific Ocean to the west, and the U.S. state Alaska to the northwest.
