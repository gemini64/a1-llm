The Villa Lario is a Renaissance patrician residence in Mandello del Lario very near Borgo of Lierna on the shores of Lake Como in northern Italy. Since 2015 the complex has been designated as a 5-star luxury hotel, equipped with helipad and seaplane landing.
In the hotel there is the Italian Chef Enrico Derflingher (2 Michelin stars), former Chef at the White House for George Bush Senior and at Buckingham palace for the Queen of England and Prince Charles of Wales.
