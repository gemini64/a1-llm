Prince Edward Island is a province in southeastern Canada. In 2015, it had 146,455 people. (*)
Located on an island in the Gulf of St. Lawrence, the province is the smallest in Canada.
Charlottetown is the provincial capital and largest city.
Prince Edward Island was the island in Anne of Green Gables, a 1908 novel from Lucy Maud Montgomery that has then been adapted several times as movies and television series.
