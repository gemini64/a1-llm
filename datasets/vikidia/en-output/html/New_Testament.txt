The New Testament (*)  (NT) is the second division of the Biblical canon.
It includes 260 chapters and 7957 verses.

Books
The New Testament includes 27 books:

Four canonical gospels: Matthew, Mark, Luke and John
The Acts of the Apostles
13 Pauline epistles
The Epistle to the Hebrews
7 general epistles
The Book of Revelation
