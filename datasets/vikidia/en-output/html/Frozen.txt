Frozen is a 3D computer-animated musical movie very loosely based on the fairy tale "The Snow Queen" by Hans Christian Andersen. The movie was released in November 2013 by Walt Disney Animation Studios. Voice actors include Kristen Bell, Idina Menzel, Jonathan Groff, Josh Gad, and Santino Fontana.

Plot
The movie is about a princess who sets off on a journey with a mountain man, his pet reindeer, and a snowman to find her sister.

Awards
The public and critics praised the movie. It won two Academy Awards, one for Best Animated Film and the other for Best Song ("Let It Go"). It won other awards, including a Golden Globe and a BAFTA.
