The area where Swedish is spoken
Swedish (Svenska) is a North Germanic language spoken in Sweden and some parts of Finland. Swedish is intelligible with Norwegian and mostly with Danish. In written language the three are more intelligible than spoken. Swedish is spoken by circa 10 million people.
