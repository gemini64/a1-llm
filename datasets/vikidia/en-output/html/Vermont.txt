Vermont is a state in the northeastern United States. In 2015, it had 626,042 people. (*)
Montpelier is the state capital, and Burlington is the largest city.
Vermont is bordered by the Canadian province Quebec to the north, New Hampshire to the east, Massachusetts to the south, and New York to the west.
From 1777 to 1791, Vermont was an independent country, the Vermont Republic, which was admitted into the union on 4 May 1791.
