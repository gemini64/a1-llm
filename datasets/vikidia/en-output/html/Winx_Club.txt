Winx Club is an animated series created by Iginio Straffi and his company Rainbow SRL later seasons also by  Viacom USA.

English Language versions
In English language there are a lot of versions produced by many studios.

Cinelume or Rai English : dubbed seasons 1,2,3,4 and also the movies to the original Italian language dialogue.



4Kids Entertainment : censored Winx Club seasons 1,2,3 for very young fans. They also worked for the video games : Winx (videogame), Mission Enchantix and The Quest for The codex.



Nickelodeon USA  dubbed seasons 3-4 and produced seasons 5, 6 and the tv specials. Recorded by Atlas Oceanic.



Duart recorded seasons 7 & 8 and also world of Winx.
