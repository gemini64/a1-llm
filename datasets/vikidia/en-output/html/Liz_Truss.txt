Mary Elizabeth Truss (born 26 July 1975) is a British politician who served as Prime Minister of the United Kingdom from 6 September to 25 October 2022. She was Leader of the Conservative Party from 5 September to 25 October 2022. She was the shortest-serving prime minister in the United Kingdom's history. She was succeeded by Rishi Sunak.
She was MP for South West Norfolk from 6 May 2010 until 30 May 2024, when she lost her seat to Labour politician Terry Jermy in the 2024 general election.
