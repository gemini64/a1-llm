Novak Djokovic in 2011
Novak Djokovic is a Serbian tennis player.
He was born in Belgrade on May 22, 1987.
He has been a pro player since 2003.
He is currently third in the world in the Association of Tennis Professionals rankings. Previously he has been first for 223 weeks.
His coaches are Dušan Vemić and Marian Vajda. Since 2016, Boris Becker is no longer his coach.
