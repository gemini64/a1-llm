Sebastian Vettel is a retired German Formula 1 driver. Born on July 3, 1987, in Heppenheim, Germany, Vettel made his F1 debut in 2006 with BMW Sauber before moving to Toro Rosso, where he won his first race in 2008. He then joined Red Bull Racing, where he became one of the most dominant drivers in F1 history, winning four consecutive World Championships from 2010 to 2013. Vettel later raced for Ferrari from 2015 to 2020 before retiring from F1 at the end of the 2022 season with Aston Martin.
