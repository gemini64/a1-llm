North Dakota is a state in the midwestern United States. In 2015, it had 756,927 people. (*)
Bismarck is the state capital, and Fargo is the largest city.
North Dakota is bordered by the Canadian provinces Manitoba and Saskatchewan to the north, Minnesota to the east, South Dakota to the south, and Montana to the west.
