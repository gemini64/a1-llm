The Union Jack
Union Jack is the popular name of the Union Flag, which is the national flag of the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland. It is a combination of the flags of England, Scotland and Ireland.

Flag of England : Cross of St.George (patron saint of England) red on white ground. Adopted in 800.
Flag of Scotland : Cross of St. Andrew (patron saint of Scotland) white on blue ground. Added in 1707
Flag of Ireland : Cross of St.Patrick (patron saint of Ireland) red on white ground. Added in 1801.
