Sunni Muslim men in Bandar Abbas, Iran, celebrating Eid al-Fitr with a festive meal
Eid al-Fitr is an Islamic holiday that marks the end of the month of fasting Ramadan. Bajram is usually congratulated by Bosniaks as follows: Bajram sherif mubarek olsun. In translation: "May the noble Eid be blessed." The answer is as follows: Allah razi olsun, in translation: "May Allah be pleased." Eid al-Fitr is an Islamic holiday celebrated for the end of Ramadan. For Eid al-Fitr, one often fasts, renouncing all bodily pleasures, smoking, drinking, food, and sexual intercourse.
