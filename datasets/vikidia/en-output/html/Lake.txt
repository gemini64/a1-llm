A lake is a body of water. Lakes can be freshwater (water without salt) lakes or saltwater lakes.
The largest lake in the world is the Caspian Sea of Asia, and the largest freshwater lake is Lake Superior of North America. The deepest lake is Lake Baikal of Asia. The lowest lake is the Dead Sea of Asia, and the highest lake that can be navigated by boats is Lake Titicaca of South America.
