Sons of the Desert is a comedy movie starring Stan Laurel and Oliver Hardy. It was released in December 1933. In 2012, the film was deemed "culturally, historically, or aesthetically significant" by the United States Library of Congress and selected for preservation in the National Film Registry. The movie is about two married men who try to outwit their wives over a trip to a party-like, men-only, out-of-town "Sons of the Desert" convention.
