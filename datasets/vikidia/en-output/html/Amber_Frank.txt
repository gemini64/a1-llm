Amber Dawn Frank (December 2, 1998) (*) , formerly known professionally as Amber Montana, is an American child actress, best known for her lead role of Taylor Hathaways in the Nickelodeon series, The Haunted Hathaways.

Early years and career
Montana was born in Tampa, Florida. She has Colombian ascendancy by her mother. When she was 8 years old, she moved to Los Angeles to become an actress. In 2008, she obtained the role of a kidnapped child in the TV movie She Could Be You. From that moment, Amber decided to pursue her acting career, obtaining important roles in the following years. In 2012 she participated in the Nickelodeon game show Brain Surge, and one year later the lead role in The Haunted Hathaways. (*)
