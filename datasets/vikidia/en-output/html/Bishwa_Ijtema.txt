Biswa Ijtema, or Biswa Ijtima, is usually any major global gathering every year, specifically the annual international gathering of the Tablighi Jamaat, held on the Turag River banks in Tongir, Bangladesh. This gathering of Tablighi Jamaat is the largest in the world and is attended by devout Muslims worldwide. Usually, this gathering is organized every year in winter; for this reason, the month of December or January is chosen.
