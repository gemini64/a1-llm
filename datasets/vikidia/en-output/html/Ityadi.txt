Ityadi is a popular television magazine program in Bangladesh. Etc. The first publication was in 1989. Hanif Sanket is the presenter of this humorous satirical program which is aired every three months on Bangladesh Television.

Story
Launched in the 1990s, the show has become one of the most popular shows in Bangladesh. The show has been airing for almost 30 years. However, at present it is broadcast on Bangladesh Television every three months. Usually a new etc. is broadcast on the fifth Friday of the month after the Bangla news at 8 pm and compiled etc. on the first Sunday of every month after the English news at 10 pm In addition, the program is broadcast every year after the English news at 10 pm on the day after Eid-ul-Fitr.
