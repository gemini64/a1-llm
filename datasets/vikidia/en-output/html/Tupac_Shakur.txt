Lesane Parish Crooks (later Tupac Amaru Shakur), better known as Tupac Shakur, Tupac, or 2Pac, was an American rapper and actor. He was born in July 1971 in New York City and died in September 1996 in Las Vegas. He is known to be one of the greatest rappers in the world, continuing to sell many records after his death. He also acted in 8 movies.
