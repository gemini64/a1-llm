Wikiquote is a wiki hosted by the Wikimedia Foundation. It has a collection of quotations from people, works of literature, and media. As of February 2015, there were over 24,800 articles on the English Wikiquote. (*)
The Simple English wikiquote was officially locked in 2009, with people seeing that not enough people were contributing. (*)
