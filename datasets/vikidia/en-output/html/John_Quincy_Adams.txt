Photograph of John Quincy Adams
John Quincy Adams (1767-1848) was the sixth president of the United States, serving one term from 1825 to 1829. He was preceded by James Monroe and was succeeded by Andrew Jackson. John Quincy Adams was the son of the second president John Adams.
