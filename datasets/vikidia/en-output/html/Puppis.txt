Puppis is a constellation that can be seen in the southern sky. (*)  It is one of the 88 modern constellations recognized by the International Astronomical Union.


Notable features

Argo Navis was sub-divided in 1752 by the French astronomer Nicolas Louis de Lacaille, forming Puppis.
