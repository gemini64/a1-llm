Augustus Gloop is a character in Charlie and the Chocolate Factory novel. He is very fat and eat's at least twenty Wonka bars each day! He is the first one who found a golden ticket. In Wonka's factory he is so greedy that he falls in the chocolate river and is sucked up the pipe to the fudge room. An Opma-Loompa shows Augustus parents to the Fudge where they take out Augustus from the machines.
