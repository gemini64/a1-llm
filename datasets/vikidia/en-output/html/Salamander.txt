Salamander
Salamander is a lizard-like animal. It is an animal that lives in wetlands.
The salamander eats worms. It is coloured. It has spots all over the body. It is slimy. It is not a reptile. It is a Amphibian

Scientific denomination and classification

Its scientific name is Caudata.

Behaviour
They eat worms. You can see it when it rains or when there is humidity.

Habitat
She lives in wet areas.

Evolution
They descend from the dinosaurs.
