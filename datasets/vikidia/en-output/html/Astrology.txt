Astrology is the belief that the positions of objects in the solar system can have an affect on human personality and lives, especially when it comes to the time and place of birth of someone. Some astrologers may also use it as a method of predicting the future.

Western astrological signs



In addition, Astrology may also include signs for the planets of the Solar system, the Moon and Pluto, as well as things such as a "Part of Fortune", a True Node, and a Rising sign. These are determined by the exact date, time and place of your birth however.
