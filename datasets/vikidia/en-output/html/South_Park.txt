The logo of South ParkSouth Park is an adult animated sitcom created by Trey Parker and Matt Stone, that airs on Comedy Central since August 13 1997. It has 26 seasons.
The sitcom talks about 4 kids : Stan Marsh, Kyle Broflovski, Eric Cartman and Kenny McCormick that all live in South Park, a city in Colorado.
South Park's humor is absurd, parodic, sarcastic, gravelly and scatologic and often talks about sensitive topics such as death or current controversial topics.
South Park often causes controversies because of certain episodes.
