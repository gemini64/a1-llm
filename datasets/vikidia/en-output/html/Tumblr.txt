Tumblr is a microblogging and social networking site. It was founded by David Karp in February 2007 and currently owned by American company Automattic.
The CEO is Matt Mullenweg.
On 21 October 2011, then US President Barack Obama created a Tumblr account.

Parent companies

Yahoo! Inc. (2013–2017)
Oath (2017–2019)
Verizon Media (2019)
Automattic (2019–present)
