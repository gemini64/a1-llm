Rajshahi Division
Rajshahi Division, situated in the northwestern part of Bangladesh, is a testament to the rich cultural heritage, historical significance, and diverse landscapes that define this region. Comprising eight districts, namely Rajshahi, Bogura, Pabna, Natore, Sirajganj, Joypurhat, Chapainawabganj, and Naogaon, this division is a focal point for the confluence of tradition and progress.
