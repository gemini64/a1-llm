The Canyoning is an extreme sport. It is about travelling in a canyon, combining various means and techniques, from walking and swimming to climbing, rapelling jumping and navigating.
It is a dangerous sport due to the nature of some of those techniques and physical condition required to go through some difficult slots, as well as due to the natural conditions: those may include intense sun and hot air, cold water and dangerous currents in the river, abrasive canyon walls etc.
