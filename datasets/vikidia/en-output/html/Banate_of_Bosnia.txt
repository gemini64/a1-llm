The Banate of Bosnia was a medieval state in Europe. It was founded in the 12th century and existed until the coronation of Tvrtko I, after which it became a kingdom. It was first metioned by Constantine VII Porphyrogenitus in his work De administrando imperio, where he wrote "Horion Bosona," meaning "the land of Bosnia"  or "tiny Bosnia / "small land of Bosnia".
