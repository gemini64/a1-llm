South Carolina is a state in the southeastern United States. In 2015, it had 4,896,146 people. (*)
Columbia is the state capital and the largest city.
South Carolina is bordered by North Carolina to the north, the Atlantic Ocean to the southeast, and Georgia to the southwest.
