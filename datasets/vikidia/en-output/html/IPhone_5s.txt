iPhone 5s
The iPhone 5s is a smartphone made by Apple Inc. It was first released in 2013 along with the iPhone 5c. It was succeeded by the iPhone 6 and iPhone 6 Plus in 2014.
The device originally came with iOS 7.0, and the latest available version is iOS 9.2.
New features compared to the iPhone 5 include Touch ID (a fingerprint recognition system) and a 64-bit processor.
