Tennessee is a state in the southeastern United States. As of 2015, it had 6,600,299 people. (*)
Nashville is the state capital, but Memphis is the most populous city. Other large cities include Knoxville and Chattanooga.
Tennessee is bordered by Kentucky to the north, Virginia to the northeast, North Carolina to the east, Georgia to the southeast, Alabama to the south, Mississippi and Arkansas to the southwest, and Missouri to the northwest.
