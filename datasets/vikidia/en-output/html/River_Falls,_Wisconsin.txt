River Falls is a city in Pierce County, Wisconsin and St. Croix County, Wisconsin. Most of the city is located within the Town of River Falls in Pierce County. A small part lies within the Town of Kinnickinnic in St. Croix County. River Falls has the largest population of any city in Pierce county. The population was 15,000 at the 2010 census. (*)  It has 11,851 people living in Pierce County, and 3,149 in St. Croix County. It is part of the Minneapolis-St. Paul metropolitan area.
