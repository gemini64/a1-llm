A decimal is the part of the number that is smaller than 1 whole. They are seperated by the decimal point which looks like this ..We use decimals for money when we talk cents (0.01) in money.

Decimal place value

0.1 (1 tenth). It is the first place value to the right of the decimal point.
0.01 (1 hundredth). Its the second place value to the right of the decimal and ten times smaller than a tenth.
0.001 (1 thousandth). Thousandths are ten times smaller than hundredths and the prefix milli means 1 thousandth. They are the 3rd place value to the right of the decimal point.
0.0001 (1 ten thousandth). Ten thousandths are the 4th place value to the right of the decimal point.
