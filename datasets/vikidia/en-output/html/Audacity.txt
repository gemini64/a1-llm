Audacity's logo
Audacity is a free audio editing software. It can be used to edit many types of audio file formats, such as WAV, AIFF, MP3, and OGG.
Audacity is both free in price and free in copyright. It is also open source, which means anyone can look at its source code.
The software is cross-platform, available for Microsoft Windows, Mac OS X, Linux, and more.
