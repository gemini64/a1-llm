Dr. Mohammad Azam is an assistant professor of Department of Bangla in University of Dhaka. (*)  He is the Director general of Bangla Academy. (*)

Biography
Dr. Mohammad was born in Noakhali District on August 23, 1975. He completed his study and received doctorate degree from University of Dhaka. (*)
