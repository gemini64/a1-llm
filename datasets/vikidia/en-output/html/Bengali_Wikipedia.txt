The Bengali version of the open online encyclopedia, Wikipedia, is the Bengali Wikipedia. It was launched on January 27, 2004. The Bangla Media 10,000 portals milestone was achieved in October 2006, and 1 lakh portals on March 25, 2020. As of January 30, 2024, Bengali Wikipedia has created 146,556 portals and has been accessed 71,76,169 times. Despite being launched after some of the top Wikipedia sections, it still ranks 5th overall in port depth among the 318 active Wikipedia in the language.
