Perman, also known as PÄman in Japan, is a popular manga series created by Fujiko Fujio. It was first serialized in Weekly Shonen Sunday in 1967. The series was later adapted into anime, with the first black and white anime series released in 1967 and a second colour anime series released in 1983. Several films were released in 1983, 1984, 1985, 2003, and 2004. The anime series is currently being aired on the Super Hungama channel in India.
