Rahart Adams (February 1, 1996) is an Australian actor. He is best known for his roles in Nickelodeon series Every Witch Way as Jax Novoa and Sam Conte in Nowhere Boys.

Biography
Adams was born in Melbourne, Australia. His parents are of Maltese heritage (*) .
He began auditioning for TV commercials for different television networks such as ABC3, WIN Television and ABC 4 kids. In 2013, he finally obtained a main character role in the fantasy TV series Nowhere Boys; Adams was also elected to participate in the second season of Every Witch Way. And now he has the role of Davis In the Nickelodeon movie liar liar vampire
