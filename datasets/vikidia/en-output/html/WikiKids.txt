WikiKids is a Dutch wiki website for children of 8 to 15 years. The website has existed since 29 January 2006.  WikiKids is hosted by Stichting Kennisnet.
As of 11 May 2022, WikiKids had 35,500 articles and 245,424 registered users. (*)
