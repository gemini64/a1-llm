Amar Sonar Bangla (Bengali: আমার সোনার বাংলা, English: My Golden Bengal) is the national anthem of Bangladesh. (*)  It was written by Rabindranath Tagore, a famous poet, in 1905. This song talks about how much people love Bangladesh, its beautiful nature, and its rich culture.
