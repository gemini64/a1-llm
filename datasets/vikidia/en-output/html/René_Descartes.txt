René Descartes (31 March 1596 – 11 February 1650) was a French philosopher, scientist and mathematician.

Biography
Descartes was born on 31 March 1596 in La Haye en Touraine, Touraine, Kingdom of France to Joachim Descartes and Jeanne Descartes née. Jeanne died in May 1597, a few days after giving birth to a still-born child. Joachim was a member of the Parlememt of Rennes at Rennes.
On 1 February 1650, Descartes got pneumonia and died on 11 February 1650.
