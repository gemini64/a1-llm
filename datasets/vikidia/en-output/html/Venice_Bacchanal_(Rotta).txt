Venice Bacchanal  or Baccanale Chioggiotto is an oil on canvas (134 cm x 193 cm) made in 1900 by the painter Italian Antonio Rotta. The work, coming from the Superintendency for Artistic and Historical Heritage of Piedmont from the collection of the National Museum Galleria Sabauda in Turin, has been placed in the collection of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and has been on permanent display since 1927 at the Italian Embassy of Portugal in Lisbon located in the Palace dei Conti di Pombeiro.
