Khujoweb.com is a Bangla-search engine that is for the Bangla-speaking community. (*)  (*)  It is tailored to focus on Bangladeshi and Bengali content, ensuring that users can find local, relevant information with ease.


History
It was launched in 2025 for users in Bangladesh and the Bengali-speaking community worldwide. (*)
