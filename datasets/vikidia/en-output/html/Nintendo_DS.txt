Nintendo DS
The Nintendo DS is a handheld gaming console developed and made by Nintendo. It's known for its unique dual-screen design, with the bottom screen featuring touch input. All Nintendo DS models combined have sold 154.02 million units, making it the best-selling Nintendo system, the best-selling handheld game console, and the second-best-selling video game console of all time, overall, only about a million units behind Sony's PlayStation 2.
