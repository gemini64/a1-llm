Bida is a city and local government area in Niger State. (*)  It is the second populated city in Niger State having 260,700 people living in the community.

Kingdom
The community has an history of now known as Bida Emirate having Etsu Nupe as they called their (king) or emir as well. The kingdom was founded by Tseode in early 15 century's, Tseode an idah prince born to a family igala father but a Nupe mothers from Bida.  (*)
