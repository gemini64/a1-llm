Chadwick Boseman (born November 29, 1976 - August 28, 2020) was a famous actor. He was best known for playing the superhero Black Panther in Marvel movies and starred in several biographical movies. He portrayed Jackie Robinson in the 2013 sports movie 42 and Thurgood Marshall in the 2017 courtroom movie Marshall.
Boseman was born in Anderson, South Carolina. He died on August 28, 2020, after a 4-year battle with cancer.
