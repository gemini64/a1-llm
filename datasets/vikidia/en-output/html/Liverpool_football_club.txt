Liverpool F.C. was created in 1892 in Anfield, Liverpool after Everton moved to Goodison park and a new club was created, eventually being called Liverpool F.C. Liverpool is the second most successful English club with 65 trophies, one behind Man.United with 66. Liverpool were the most frequent winners of the First Division when it was the top flight of English football, winning it a total of 18 times before the premier league was born. since the inception of the premier league, Liverpool have won the title once, in the 2019-2020 season. Liverpool have also won the European cup (champion's league) 6 times, 1 FIFA world cup, 2 women's super league titles, 7 FA cups, and 8 league cups.
