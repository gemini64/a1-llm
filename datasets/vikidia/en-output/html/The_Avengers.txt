The Avengers is an American film released in 2001 which tells the adventures of a group of super heroes.

Summary
Nick Fury, the director of SHIELD enlists a group of superheroes: Black Widow, Captain America, Iron Man, and Hulk to find the tesseract, a cube of energy that was stolen by Loki, a god hailing from the nine worlds of Asgard. Using his scepter, Loki bewitched a group of people (including Clint Barton (Hawkeye), Black Widow's friend) and forced them to obey his orders.
After many adventures, the Avengers will manage to defeat Loki and recover the tesseract.
