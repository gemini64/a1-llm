Common raven (Corvus corax)
Ravens are birds in the Corvidae family. There are 21 species. Some ravens are capable of making and using tools.

Description
Ravens have black plumage, and some species have a white neck and torso. They have brown or blue eyes.

Diet
Ravens are omnivorous. They eat birds, insects, carrion, eggs, earthworms, fruits, nuts, rodents and more rarely fish.
