The Roman Kingdom was the earliest period of Roman history when Rome was ruled by kings. It begun in 753 BC with the legendary king and founder of Rome, Romulus. It ended in 509 BC with the overthrow of Lucius Tarquinius Superbus, the last Roman king.

List of Kings

Romulus (753–716 BC)
Numa Pomilius (715–672 BC)
Tullus Hostilius (672–640 BC)
Ancus Marcius (640–616 BC)
L. Tarquinius Priscus (616–578 BC)
Servius Tullius (578–534 BC)
L. Tarquinius Superbus (534–509 BC)
