The FIFA Football series are football games created and developed by EA (or EA Sports, thus explaining the famous EA Sports, it's in the game catchphrase before the main screen of every FIFA game), starting from 1996 and currently underway. The last game having currently appeared is FIFA 15, at the end of September 2014. The principal rival is the Pro Evolution Soccer of Konami, while the latter had less popularity since 2008.
