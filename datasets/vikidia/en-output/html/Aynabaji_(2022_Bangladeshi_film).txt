Aynabaji (Bengali: আয়নাবাজি) is a crime thriller movie from Bangladesh that was released in 2022. It was written and directed by Amitabh Reza Chowdhury. The lead role of Ayna, a talented impersonator, was played by Chanchal Chowdhury. The movie is centred around Ayna's involvement in a complicated and dangerous situation, which includes murder, deception, and intrigue, after accepting high-profile impersonation jobs.
