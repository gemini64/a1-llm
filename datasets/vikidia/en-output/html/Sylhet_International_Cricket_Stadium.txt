Sylhet International Cricket Stadium is an international cricket ground located in Sylhet, Bangladesh. (*)  It is surrounded by hills, making it a very scenic place to watch cricket matches. (*)

History

The stadium was built in 2007 and was originally called Sylhet Divisional Stadium. It was later renamed to Sylhet International Cricket Stadium. The stadium was expanded in 2013 to host international matches.  (*)
