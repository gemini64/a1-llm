The 2016 United States presidential election was the 58th quadrennial (every 4 years) presidential election in the United States.
On 8 November 2016, Republican candidate Donald Trump and his running-mate Mike Pence beat Democrat candidate Hillary Clinton and her running-mate Tim Kaine despite Trump being expected to lose.
Trump won the election despite losing the popular vote because he won more states than Hillary Clinton, this election was the fifth in which the winner lost the popular vote.
