Pamela Anderson
Pamela Anderson is a Canadian actress and model that was born in 1967.
In the 1990s, she became very popular acting in series and movies in which she used her physical attractiveness, especially the television series Baywatch, where she played a lifeguard. She also starred in the action movie Barb Wire, which was not very successful and hurt her acting career.
She is vegan and an advocate for animals.
