Ennead is the Egyptian God's own council consisting of 9 members all worshipped at Heliopolis.

Members
Atum - the finisher of the World
Shu - God of Peace, Air and Wind
Tefnut - Goddess of Air and Rain, Wife of Shu
Geb - God of the Earth, Father of Osiris
Nut - Goddess of the Sky, Mother of Osiris
Osiris - God of the Dead and Rebirth, Former king of the Egyptian Gods
Isis - Mother Goddess, Husband to Osiris
Set - Evil Brother to Osiris, God of Chaos
Nepthys - Mother Goddess, Sister of Isis
Horus - Prince of the Egyptian gods, God of the Sky and Kings
