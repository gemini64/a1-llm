A singer during her concert
A singer is a musician who uses his voice as an instrument.
A singer can sing alone, so it is a soloist, with another person, it is a duo, and if there are three it will be a trio.
A singer can also sing with musicians who accompany him, this is called a music group.
Depending on the pitch of the voice, male singers are classified as tenor or bass, female singers as alto or soprano.
