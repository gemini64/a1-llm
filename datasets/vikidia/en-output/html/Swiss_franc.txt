One franc
The Swiss franc is the official monetary unit of Switzerland and Liechtenstein. De franc is divided in 100 rappen. There are coins with the value 5, 10, 20 and 50 rappen and 1, 2 and 5 franc. There are bank notes with the value 10, 20, 50, 100, 200 and 1000 franc. On every bank note there is a famous Swiss pictured.
