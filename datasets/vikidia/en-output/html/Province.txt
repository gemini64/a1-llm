A province is a section or chunk of land in a country that divides itself into sections.
For example, the country of Canada is divided into provinces and territories.
All of the following countries are also divided into provinces: Afghanistan, Algeria, Argentina, Armenia, Belarus, Belgium, Bolivia, Bulgaria, Cambodia, Chile, China, Costa Rica, Colombia, Cuba, Dominican Republic, Ecuador, Equatorial Guinea, Fiji, Finland, Gabon, Greece, Indonesia, Iran, Ireland, Italy, Kazakhstan, Kenya, Kyrgyzstan, Laos, Madagascar, Netherlands, South Korea, Norway, Pakistan, Panama, Papua New Guinea, Peru, Philippines, Poland, Rwanda, São Tomé, Saudi Arabia, Solomon Islands, South Africa, Spain, Sri Lanka, Tajikistan, Thailand, Turkey, Turkmenistan, Ukraine, Uzbekistan, Vanuatu, Vietnam, Zambia, and Zimbabwe.
