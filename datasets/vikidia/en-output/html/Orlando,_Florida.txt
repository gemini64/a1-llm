Orlando is a city in Orange County in Florida. It includes theme parks like Walt Disney World, Seaworld, and Universal Studios.
Orlando has a large, busy airport. The Orlando International Airport. Another airport near the city is Orlando Sanford International Airport. This is smaller and located further from the city center.
