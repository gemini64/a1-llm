Chicago is the most populous city in Illinois and the third most populous in the United States (after New York City and Los Angeles). In 2013, the population of the city proper was 2,718,782. (*)  In 2011, the urban area had 9.676 million people, which makes it the third largest urban area in the United States. (*)
