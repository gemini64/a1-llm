Michael Jordan is a former basketball player and businessman. He was born in Brooklyn, New York in 1963. He lead the NBA basketball team, the Chicago Bulls, to win 6 NBA final titles. After the Bulls won the 1998 NBA finals, Jordan retired from basketball. This was not the first time Jordan retired: he also retired about 1993 to play baseball. He then returned to basketball with the Washington Wizards in 2001 until his 3rd retirement in 2003. He did not win any NBA finals with the Wizards. Sports people then retired Jordan's number, 23 which included the Bulls, Wizards, and the Miami Heat. Jordan played at the University of North Carolina as a basketball player. He now owns many businesses and has his own shoe brand.
