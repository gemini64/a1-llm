The Pound is the name for a common unit of currency used in different countries around the world.  Each of the 18 countries which use the pound,  has its own version and they're (mostly) not interchangeable (you can't use one country's version of the Pound in a different country).  The Pound in its different forms, exists in :



Ascension

Egypt

The Falkland Islands

Gibraltar

Guernsey

The Isle of Man

Jersey

Lebanon

Saint Helena

South Sudan

Sudan

Syria

Tristan da Cunha

United Kingdom (Including: British Antarctic Territory, British Indian Overseas Territory, & South Georgia / Sandwich Islands).
