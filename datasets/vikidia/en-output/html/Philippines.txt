The Philippines are a country in Asia. It has 7,641 islands (*) , the biggest islands are: Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao. It is located at the western Pacific Ocean. The capital is Manila.  It is part of ASEAN.

History

And revolted against the Spanish empire, By The Philippines Revolution.  A truce was made. (*)  But after the United States captured the Philippines.
