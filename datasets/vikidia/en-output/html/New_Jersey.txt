New Jersey is a state in the northeastern United States. In 2015, it had 8,958,013 people. (*)
Trenton is the state capital, and Newark is the largest city.
New Jersey is bordered by New York to the north, the Atlantic Ocean to the east, Delaware to the southwest, and Pennsylvania to the west.
