Sunderland Association Football Club is a professional football club based in the city of Sunderland, Tyne and Wear, England. The club competes in the EFL Championship, the second tier in the English football league system.
The club is located at the Stadium of Light. The club is owned by Kyril Louis-Dreyfus and Juan Sartori. Kyril Louis-Dreyfus is the chairman and the Head Coach is Régis Le Bris.
The club was founded in 1879 and played in the Premier League from 1996–1997, 1999–2003, 2005–2006, and 2007–2017.
