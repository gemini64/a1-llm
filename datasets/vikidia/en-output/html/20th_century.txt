The 20th century is the century from the years CE 1901 to CE 2000.

Major events

The 20th century saw two world wars (World War I, World War II), man's first flight in space and major advances in medicine, technology and science. (*)



Significant persons

Martin Luther King, Jr. (1929-1968) has used non-violent civil disobedience to improve civil rights.
