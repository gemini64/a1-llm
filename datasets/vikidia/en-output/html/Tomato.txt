A tomato is an edible berry that is both a fruit and a vegetable.  For the purpose of preparing food, they are considered to be a vegetable, but in the plant kingdom, they are actually a fruit.  Tomato plants are vines.  Tomatoes can be used as sauces for pizzas and pasta,  as well as ingredients in tacos, burritos, curries and many other foods.  They can also be eaten raw.
Some types of tomato plant are attacked and damaged, or eaten by insects.
