Hayley Mills (b. 18 April 1946) is an English actress.
She is the daughter of actor John Mills.
She began her movie career as a child with Tiger Bay in 1959. She went on to international fame in 6 movies over 6 years for Walt Disney: Pollyanna, The Parent Trap, In Search of the Castaways, Summer Magic, The Moon Spinners, and That Darn Cat!.
She won the last awarded Academy Juvenile Award for Pollyanna. Other notable movies include Whistle Down the Wind (1961) with Alan Bates, and The Chalk Garden (1964) with Deborah Kerr and John Mills.
