The Meuse is a river that begins in France passes trough Belgium and ends in the Netherlands. It's 950 km long.

The course of the Meuse
The Meuse starts in France at 409 m of altitude and ends in The Netherlands at the North Sea. The river ends in parallel with the river Rhin.
