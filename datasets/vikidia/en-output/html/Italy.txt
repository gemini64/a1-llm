Location of Italy in Europe
Italy is a country in southern Europe.
Italy is a member state of the European Union and a member state of the Eurozone.  Its capital is Rome.  Italy has borders with Switzerland, France and Austria.  It also has the Alps that are somewhere between the border of Italy and Switzerland.
