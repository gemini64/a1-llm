The albatross is the biggest bird in the marine world. Its wings are white with a light orange beak.The female albatross makes eggs. The albatross is called a sea bird. The Albatross flies.



Scientific denomination and classification
ALBATROSS
The scientific name of Albatross is Diomedeide.



Behaviour
The Albatross eat fish and krill.
The Albatross live in couples.



Habitat
The Albatross lives in the Pacific Ocean.



Evolution
ALBATROSS OF TRISTAN
The descend of Albatross is Albatross of Tristan.
