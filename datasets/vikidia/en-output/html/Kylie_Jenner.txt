Kylie Jenner
Kylie Jenner (born on August 10, 1997, in Los Angeles, California) is an American reality TV personality, actress, fashion designer, businesswoman, and Instagram influencer with several million followers. She is also Kendall Jenner's sister. Kylie Jenner is known for her appearances on Keeping Up With the Kardashians.
She and Kendall won the prize for the most elegant sisters. Then in 2013, they launched their own clothing line at PacSun. Kylie said she quit her modeling career to focus on fashion. She parades during New York Fashion Week for the rapper Kanye West. In February 2015, she made the cover of Fault magazine.
In March 2019, at the age of 21, she became the youngest billionaire entrepreneur in the world, and the New York Post called her "the most influential celebrity in the fashion industry". Her father was an athlete before becoming a transgender woman by the name of Caitlyn Jenner.
