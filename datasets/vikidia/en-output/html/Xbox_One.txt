Xbox  One
The Xbox One is a gaming console developed by Microsoft and was released on 22, November 2013. A key part of the Xbox One experience is Xbox Network. Connecting your system online to Xbox Network allows you to buy game downloads and watch videos, share your recorded gameplay videos, use Skype to talk to friends and family, keep track of your friends, achievements, and game progress. The Xbox One has sold 57.96 million units lifetime. The Xbox Series X and S is 30.73 million units behind lifetime Xbox One sales.
The Xbox One superseded the Xbox 360.
