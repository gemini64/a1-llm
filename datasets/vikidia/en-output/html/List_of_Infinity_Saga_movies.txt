The Infinity Saga is what the story that spans 23 movies is called within the MCU. It started at 2008 and continued onward to 2019. This saga is about the Avengers  and their adventures involving the Infinity Stones which puts them in an inevitable collision course with the villain Thanos. This is also the saga that SETS UP the Marvel Cinematic Universe setting the origins for many of the characters that show up and the journeys they go throughout the years.

The Infinity Saga

Phase 1 focuses on setting up the Marvel universe as well as showing the origins of the more major players of the entire MCU. It also builds up to the formation of The Avengers and their initial battle against Loki
