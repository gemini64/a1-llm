The iPhone 6 Plus is a smartphone made by Apple Inc. It was first released in 2014 along with the iPhone 6.
The device originally came with iOS 8.0, and the latest available version is iOS 9.2.
New features compared to the iPhone 5s include a much larger display and near field communication (NFC). The main difference between the iPhone 6 and iPhone 6 Plus is that the iPhone 6 Plus has a larger display.
