Ub Drawing Mickey
'Ubbe Eert "Ub" Iwerks(born 1903,died 1971) was a cartoon drawer, animator and co-creator of Mickey Mouse. He worked for Walt Disney until he left in 1930 to work for Pat Powers, and eventually set up his own Studio, which created  ComiColor, Flip The Frog, and Willie Whopper. He died in 1971 of a Heart Attack.
