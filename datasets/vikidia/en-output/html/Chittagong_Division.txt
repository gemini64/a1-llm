Chittagong Division is one of the eight administrative divisions of Bangladesh, located in the country's southeastern part. It is the second-largest division by area and population, playing a crucial role in Bangladesh's economic and cultural landscape.

Geography
Diverse landscapes, including hills, forests, rivers, and the coastline along the Bay of Bengal, characterize the Chittagong Division. The Chittagong Hill Tracts, featuring lush green hills, form a significant part of the division.
