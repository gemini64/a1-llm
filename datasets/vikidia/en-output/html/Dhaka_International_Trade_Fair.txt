Dhaka International Trade Fair
The Dhaka International Trade Fair is a global exhibition held in Bangladesh. Manufacturers from Bangladesh, India, Pakistan, Iran, Japan, China, the United States, the United Kingdom, Australia, Germany, and other countries participate in the fair to showcase their products. The event is crucial for displaying various products, exploring export opportunities, and building relationships with local and foreign buyers.
This exhibition allows industrial and consumer goods makers to promote their products' quality, design, packaging, and other features. They can also establish business relationships with potential buyers to increase domestic and international trade.
The fair is held annually in January and is a significant event for businesses worldwide.
