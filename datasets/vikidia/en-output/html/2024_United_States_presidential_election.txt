The 2024 United States presidential election is the 60th presidential election, which will be held on 5 November 2024. The election elected a president and vice president for a term of four years.
The election was won by Republican Donald Trump and his running mate JD Vance against Democratic former Vice President Kamala Harris and her running mate Tim Walz.
Robert F. Kennedy Jr., the nephew of President John F. Kennedy ran in the election as an independent candidates but later dropped out and endorsed Trump.
