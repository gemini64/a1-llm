XXXTentacion (real name Jahseh Dwayne Ricardo Onfroy) was an American rapper, singer, and songwriter. He was born on January 23, 1998, in Plantation, Florida, and died on June 18, 2018 (at age 20) in Deerfield Beach, Florida. 4 men were arrested for his death. His son, Gekyume Onfroy, was born on January 26th, 2019 after his death. XXXTentacion is one of the most known emo rappers.
