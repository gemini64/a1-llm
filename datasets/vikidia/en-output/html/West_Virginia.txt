West Virginia is a state in the northeastern United States. In 2015, it had 1,844,128 people. (*)
Charleston is the state capital and largest city.
West Virginia is bordered by Pennsylvania to the north, Maryland to the northeast, Virginia to the east, Kentucky to the southwest, and Ohio to the northwest.
