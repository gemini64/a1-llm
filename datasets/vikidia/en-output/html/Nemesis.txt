Nemesis was the Greek goddess of revenge, balance, and retribution. She was considered to be remorseless.
The myth Nemesis is most famous for, is that of Narcissus. It was her who had made him fall in love with himself.
Nemesis's symbols are the sword, lash, dagger, measuring rod, scales, bridle, and the goose.
Nemesis is the daughter of Nyx, the night, who was one of the first five forces to create our world today.
