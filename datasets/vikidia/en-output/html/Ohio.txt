Ohio is a state in the midwestern United States. As of 2015, it had 11,613,423 people. (*)
Columbus is the state capital and largest city. Other large cities include Cleveland, Cincinnati, and Toledo.
Ohio is bordered by Lake Erie to the north, Pennsylvania to the northeast, West Virginia to the southeast, Kentucky to the southwest, Indiana to the west, and Michigan to the northwest.
