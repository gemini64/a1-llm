Winx Club is an video game based on the first season of the animated series with the same name and published for the PC, PS2 and GBA. The game is made by Konami, DC Studios and 4Kids Entertainment and Rainbow SRL.

Content
The  player is playing Bloom (a main character) in the game and discover several locations, fighting with monsters and witches and travelling with her fairy friends and the boys from Red Fountain.

Differences

PC and PS2 versions contains 3D animation voice actors voices dialogue, which does not cointains in the GBA version where the game is 2D and not contains voice actoes voices dialogue.
The game contains things of 4Kids Entertainment who censored the Winx animated series. However the game is based on the first season of 4Kids there is differences between the game and season 1 4kids version scenes of story dialogue.
