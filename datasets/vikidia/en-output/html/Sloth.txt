Sloths are slow-moving animals that dwell in trees. Sloths constitute the suborder Folivora.

Scientific classification

Domain: Eukaryota
Kingdom: Animalia
Phylum: Chordata
Class: Mammalia
Superorder: Xenarthra
Order: Pilosa
Suborder: Folivora (Delsuc, Catzeflis, Stanhope, and Douzery, 2001 (*)
