An uncle is the brother of one's parent.
The female equivalent is an aunt.
The uncle of one's spouse is called an uncle-in-law.
The brother of one's father is known as a "paternal uncle" while the brother of one's mother is known as a "maternal uncle".
The brother of one's grandparent is called a great uncle, the brother of one's great grandparent is a great great uncle, the brother of one's great great grandparent is one's great great great uncle and so on and so forth.
