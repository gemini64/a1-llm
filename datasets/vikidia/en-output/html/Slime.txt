Slime is a non-Newtonian fluid. It can be sticky and is often played with kids. Oobleck is another type of slime and is made with cornstarch and water.

Slime ingredients
On recipies, it shows some of these ingredients

Tide
Contact solution (contains boric acid, so it is toxic)
Saline solution (Can be made homemade or bought from the store, the store version contains boric acid)
Water (H2O)
Glue
Baking soda
Iron powder (only for metallic slime)
