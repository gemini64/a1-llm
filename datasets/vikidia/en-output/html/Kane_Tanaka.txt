Kane Tanaka (Japanese: 田中カ子; 2 January 1903 – 19 April 2022) was a Japanese supercentenarian, who became the world's oldest living person on 22 July 2018, succeeding Chiyo Miyako. At the time of her death, she was the second oldest documented person in history, after Jeanne Calment, and she is also the oldest person ever to have lived in Japan and Asia.
