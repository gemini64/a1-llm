Wisconsin is a state in the midwestern United States. In 2015, it had 5,771,337 people. (*)
Madison is the state capital, and Milwaukee is the largest city.
Wisconsin is bordered by Lake Superior and the upper peninsula of Michigan to the north, Lake Michigan to the east, Illinois to the south, Iowa to the southwest, and Minnesota to the west.
