Indiana is a state in the midwestern United States. In 2015, it had around 6,619,680 people. (*)
Indianapolis is the state capital and largest city.
Indiana is bordered by Michigan to the north, Ohio to the east, Kentucky to the south, Illinois to the west, and Lake Michigan to the northwest.
