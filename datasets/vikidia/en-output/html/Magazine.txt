A magazine is a periodical publication that is typically released on a weekly, monthly, or quarterly basis. (*)  It is designed to provide readers with a diverse range of content, including articles, photographs, advertisements, and various types of features. Magazines are usually focused on specific subjects or interests, such as fashion, technology, sports, or lifestyle. (*)
