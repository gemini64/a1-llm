Thomas Edward Patrick Brady Jr. (born August 3, 1977) is an American former professional football quarterback who played in the National Football League (NFL) for 23 seasons. He spent his first 20 seasons with the New England Patriots and was a central contributor to the franchise's 2000s, and 2010s dynasties. His final three seasons, he was a member of the Tampa Bay Buccaneers where he brought home the Super Bowl in 2021. Brady is widely regarded as one of the best quarterbacks in the games history.
