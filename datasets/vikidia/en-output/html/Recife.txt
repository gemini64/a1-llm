Recife
Recife is a city in Pernambuco, Brazil. It is located in the east along the coast of the Atlantic Ocean. In 2013, the city proper had 1,599,513 people; (*)  and in 2010, the urban area had 3,690,547 people. (*)
Recife was one of the host cities of the 2014 FIFA World Cup.
