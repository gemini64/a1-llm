Philippe Apeloig is a graphic designer who lives in France. He was born in November 1962.


Education
Apeloig studied at the École Supérieure des Arts Appliqués and the École Supérieure des Arts Décoratifs. (*)

Main body of work

Exhibitions

1988, "Philippe Apeloig, affiches", Galerie Impression; Paris
1990, "Philippe Apeloig, affiches", Centre Arc-en-Rêve, Bordeaux
1997, "Poster in the context of French culture", Galerie GGG, Tokyo
1999, "The Lingering Memory", Cooper Union School of Art, Houghton Gallery, NY
2001, "Au coeur du mot", Galerie Anatome, Paris
2005, "Typo/Typé", Carré Sainte-Anne, Montpellier; Musée d'Art Russe, Kiev
2008, "Vivo in Typo", Espace Topographique de l'art, Paris
2011, "Bewegte Schrift", exposition de groupe, Museum für Gestaltung, Zürich
2013, "Typorama", Retrospective exhibition, Les Arts Décoratifs, Paris
