Borisav Jović
Borisav Jović (19 October 1928 – 13 September 2021), is the President of the Presidency of Yugoslavia from 1990 to 1991. He was succeeded by Sejdo Bajramović.

Life
Borisav Jović was born on 19 October 1928, in Nikšić, Batočina, Kingdom of Serbs, Croats and Slovenes.

Death
Borisav Jović died from COVID-19 on 13 September 2021, in Belgrade, Serbia.
