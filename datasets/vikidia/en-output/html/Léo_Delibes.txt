Delibes in 1888
Léo Delibes was a French composer. He was born in St. Germain-du-Val, France, on 21 February 1836. He died in 1891, and was buried in the Cimetière de Montmartre, Paris.
Delibes is famous for his ballet music. He wrote La Source (with another composer), Coppélia, and Sylvia. He also wrote the opera, Lakme.
