CityVille is a video game developed by Zynga Studios. The game is possible to play through the social network Facebook. It was announced the game would start on 11 November of 2010. However, it was not released until December of 2010. The delay was due to additional adjustments to the game.
The video game is available in nine languages: Spanish, English, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Turkish and Indonesian.
