The 19th century is the century from the years CE 1801 to CE 1900.

Major events

Much of this period is also called the Victorian era. It started in 1837, when Victoria Queen of the United Kingdom began her rule and ended in 1901 when she died. (*)



Significant persons

Jules Verne (1828-1905)
Karl Marx (1818-1883)
