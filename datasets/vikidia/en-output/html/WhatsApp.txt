Whatsapp
WhatsApp Messenger, or simply Whatsapp is a cross-platform application that allows you to exchange messages and multimedia files between smartphone owners via the Internet, in addition to text messages, WhatsApp Messenger users can send each other pictures, videos, audio files, and location on a map.  WhatsApp is one of the most successful Messenger services in late 2000s to mid 2010s.
The application was originally created by the ukranian developer Jam Koum in 2009 and later on it was bought by Meta Platforms Inc. back in 2014
The name WhatsApp is a pun to turn from "What's up?" And from the acronym App.
