Tamim Iqbal Khan is a professional cricketer from Bangladesh. He started his career in One Day International matches in 2007 and played his first Test match in the same year. Tamim served as the vice-captain of the Bangladesh national cricket team from December 2010 to September 2011.

Personal Life
Tamin Iqbal Khan was born in Chattogram Bangladesh on March 20, 1989. Tamin was the first cricketer to reach 5000 ODI runs when he reached the milestone against England in October of 2016.
