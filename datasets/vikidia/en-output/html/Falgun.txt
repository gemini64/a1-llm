Falgun or Phagun is the eleventh month of the Bengali calendar, the Nepali calendar and the twelfth and final month of the Indian national century. This month marks the beginning of spring, the sixth and last season in Bangladesh, Nepal and Assam. In the Gregorian calendar, Falgun typically starts on February 13. The Bengali calendar has undergone several reforms in Bangladesh. As per the latest reforms, it has been officially declared that Falgun begins on February 14 from 2020 onwards.
