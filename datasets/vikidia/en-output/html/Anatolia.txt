Anatolia (Turkish: Anadolu), also called Asia Minor, is a large peninsula or region in Turkey, which makes up most of its territory.
Anatolia includes several ethnic groups including Turks, Kurds, Armenians, Chechens, Circassians, Greeks, and Laz.
Turkish is the most widely spoken language in Anatolia but other languages are spoken including Kurdish, Armenian, Greek, Kabardian, and the North Caucasian languages.
