New Hampshire is a state in the northeastern United States. In 2015, it had 1,330,608 people. (*)
Concord is the state capital, and Manchester is the largest city.
New Hampshire is bordered by the Canadian province Quebec to the north, Maine and the Atlantic Ocean to the east, Massachusetts to the south, and Vermont to the west.
