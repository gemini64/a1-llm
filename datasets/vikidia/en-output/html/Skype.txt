Skype is a video conferencing software bought by Microsoft. We can call anybody (video-conferencing - free - need a webcam) or call a phone number (not free), send SMS (not free), chatting (instant messaging - free), send files (free), remove ads (not free). Skype is available for computers (Mac and Windows), Xbox, smartphones (Windows Phone, Android or iPhone), tablets (Android or iPad), TVs.
