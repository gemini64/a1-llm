The 2020 United States presidential election was held on 5 November 2020 to decide the president and vice president of the United States for a four-year term.
It was held between Democratic Party candidate Joe Biden, whom served as Vice President under Barack Obama and then President Donald Trump. Joe Biden was elected President though the results of the election were contested by Trump who claimed election fraud, this lead the January 6 United States Capitol attack on 6 January 2021.
