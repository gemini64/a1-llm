Nishan Barua Protik (born 26 august 2004) is a musician, song writer and story writer from Bangladesh. He was born in Chittagong. He had a great love for music since childhood.He also loved to write stories.Everyone knows him as Protik Barua.
He wrote a lot about his life. But all his stories are about the people who left. One of them is 'This is the story of an imperfection in Protik Barua life?' . His first story is about the man who left him in love. His father was originally an army officer. Although he was raised by his family since his childhood, he loves his grandfather more. Because his grandfather was his companion in suffering.
