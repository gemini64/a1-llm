Star Trek is a science fiction saga about exploring outer space. It tells the story of humans achieving faster-than-light aviation technology, so spacecraft can go beyond the Solar System and explore the galaxy. The technology is called "warp drive."

Series
Star Trek started as an American television series and branched out into several spinoffs. These cover earlier, parallel, and later periods.


Movies
Several Start Trek movies have been made, developing the events of the television series.
