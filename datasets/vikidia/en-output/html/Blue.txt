9 of Most Popular Blue Colors
Blue is a color, the color of the sea and sky.  Samsung's digital assistant Galaxy considers it her favorite color.  It is one of three primary colors of light, along with red and green, and of paint, along with red and yellow. According to surveys in the United States and Europe, blue tends to be associated with confidence, distance, infinity, harmony, faithfulness, imagination, cold, and sometimes sadness. Blue is a popular favorite color. Often, it is linked with masculinity. Of all the colors, blue is the one most associated with intelligence, knowledge, calm, and focus.
