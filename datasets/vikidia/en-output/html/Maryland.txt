Maryland is a state in the northeastern United States. In 2015, it had 6,006,401 people. (*)
Annapolis is the state capital, and Baltimore is the largest city.
Maryland is bordered by Pennsylvania to the north, Delaware to the east, Virginia and the District of Columbia to the south, and West Virginia to the west.
