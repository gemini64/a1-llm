The Zainul Abedin Museum (also known as the Shilpacharya Zainul Abedin Sangrahashala) is an art museum located in Mymensingh, Bangladesh. (*)  Established in 1975, the museum is dedicated to the works of the renowned artist Zainul Abedin, who is often referred to as the founding father of modern art in Bangladesh.
