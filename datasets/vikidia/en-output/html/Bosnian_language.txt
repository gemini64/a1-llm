Bosnian language BS:(Bosanski jezik / Бocaнски језик) is a language primaraly spoken in Bosnia and Herzegovina. It is also predominantly spoken by Bosnians in Serbia, Montenegro, Croatia, Kosovo and North Macedonia and bosnian diaspora. It is co-offical language in Bosnia and Herzegovina and Montenegro. The first Bosnian dictionary was written in 1631 and it's one of the oldest "south slavic" languages today.
