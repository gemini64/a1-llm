Bertrand in 2022
Bertrand of Brazil (born February 2, 1941) is the current head of the Imperial House of Brazil, succeeding his brother Luiz of Orléans-Braganza on July 15, 2022.
Bertrand was Imperial Prince of Brazil from 1981 to 2022, he passed the title to his other brother Antônio of Orléans-Braganza.
