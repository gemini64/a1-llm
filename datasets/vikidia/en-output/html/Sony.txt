Sony, (ソニー株式会社 Sonī Kabushiki Gaisha in Japanese), is a company headquartered in Tokyo, Japan. One of the largest and important companies worldwide in the industry of audio and video
The company produces ton of electronics appliances, like gaming consoles, such as PlayStation, home appliances like televisions, computers, tablets etc.
The company was founded in 1946 by Masaru Ibukta and Akio Morita, administrated by Kenichiro Yoshida. during the 80's and the 90's, Sony was the leader of all the industries of technology section worldwide.
It is known for creating the Compact Disc, the "Walkman", the Handycam and the famous PlayStation.
In 2019, Sony was the 116th in Fortune Global 500
