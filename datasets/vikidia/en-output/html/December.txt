December 2009 in the Netherlands
December is the twelfth and last month of the year according to the Gregorian Calendar. In December, there are a lot of celebrations like Christmas and Hearth's Warming Eve.
The month includes 31 days.
In the northern hemisphere December is the last month of autumn. Winter begins December 22.
In the southern hemisphere December is the last month of spring. Summer begins December 22.
