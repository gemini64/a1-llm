Ekushey Book Fair
Ekushey Book Fair, also known as the Ekushe Book Fair, is a traditional fair in independent Bangladesh. It takes place annually throughout February in the Burdwan House premises of Bangla Academy and around the Burdwan House. Since 2014, the fair has expanded to Suhrawardy Udyan, across from Bangla Academy. However, a section of the fair is still held on the Bangla Academy premises.
