February is the second month of the year, according to the Gregorian Calendar. The month has 28 days, but in a leap year one day is added, making 29 days. The word "February" is derived from februum, a thing used for ritual purification in ancient days.
In the northern hemisphere February is a month of winter. In the southern hemisphere February is a month of summer.
