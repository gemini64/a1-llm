Logo of Dhaka Medical College Dhaka Medical College and Hospital (DMC) is one of the oldest and most prestigious medical institutions in Bangladesh, renowned for its contributions to medical education, healthcare, and research. Located in the heart of Dhaka, the capital city, DMC is affiliated with the University of Dhaka and is known for producing skilled healthcare professionals who serve in Bangladesh and internationally. Alongside its role in education, DMC operates a large hospital that serves a wide range of patients from diverse socio-economic backgrounds.
