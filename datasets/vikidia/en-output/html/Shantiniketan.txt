Santiniketan is a neighborhood in Bolpur, a town in Birbhum district in West Bengal, India. The neighborhood was founded by Maharshi Devendranath Tagore and further developed by his son Rabindranath Tagore. Visva Bharati University is located here. The university was established by Rabindranath Tagore, and its location there made Santiniketan a university town. In 2023, Santiniketan was included in the UNESCO World Heritage List. On Sunday, September 17, 2023, UNESCO announced the decision of the 45th session of the Committee on World Heritage Sites to be held in 2023.
