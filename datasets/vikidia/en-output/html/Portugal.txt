Location of Portugal in Europe
Portugal is a country in south western Europe.
Portugal is a member state of the European Union and a member state of the Eurozone. Portugal only has land borders with Spain. The main cities are Lisbon, Porto and Braga. It's capital is Lisbon.

History
Portugal had discovered and ruled many countries in the past. Now 8 countries speak Portuguese: Portugal, Timor-Leste, Brazil, Angola, Cape Verde, Guinea-Bissau, São Tomé and Príncipe, and Mozambique.
