Abelisaurus skull
Abelisaurus is a dinosaur from South America. It is a part of a group of meat eaters from the more famous Tyrannosaurs on the northern continents. It had an extremely powerful jaw. Scientists have found a 3 feet (1 m) long skull but they do not know how large Abelisaurus grew. It grew to be 20-26 ft.  (*)
