Kentucky is a state in the east central United States. In 2015, it had 4,425,092 people. (*)
Frankfort is the state capital, and Louisville is the largest city.
Kentucky is bordered by Indiana and Ohio to the north, West Virginia to the northeast, Virginia to the east, Tennessee to the south, Missouri to the west, and Illinois to the northwest.
