Jack-o'-lantern
Halloween is a day that kids trick or treat for candy. It happens every 31 October.

History
Before Halloween was Samhain. (*)  Samhain was a religious festival with the celt people. (*)  In Samhain people scare away bad spirits so people made scary pumpkins to scare them off.
