A Cricket ground is a ground, where the exciting game of cricket is played.

Parts of a Cricket Ground
A cricket ground make by it's few of parts.

Field
The entire area where the game is played. It’s usually grassy and oval-shaped.

Pitch and wickets
A pitch and wickets
Pitch is the important part of the ground. It’s a rectangular strip in the center where the bowler bowls the ball and the batsman tries to hit it. The pitch is 22 yards (20.12 meters) long and 10 feet (3.05 meters) wide.
Wickets, there are three wooden sticks called stumps with two small pieces of wood on top called bails. (*)
