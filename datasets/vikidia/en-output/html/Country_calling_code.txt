Country calling codes are used to identify all different countries by their international dialling codes.
For example, from Delhi, India to Mumbai, India you simply call (22) 9903-6670 but from London to Mumbai, India you use: 00 91 022 9903 6670.

Codes
Codes are divided into zones.:
Zone 1 - North American Numbering Plan
Zone 2 - Africa
Zone 3&4 - Europe
Zone 5 - Latin America
Zone 6 - Asia Pacific
Zone 7 - Former Soviet Union
Zone 8 - East Asia
Zone 9 - other Asia
For Zone 1 see List of NANP area codes.
20 - Egypt
27 - South Africa
30 - Greece
31 - Netherlands
32 - Belgium
