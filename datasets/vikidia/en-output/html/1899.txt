1899 (MDCCCXCIX) is a year.  It comes after 1898 and before 1900.  It was the 899th year of the second millenium, and concluded both the 1890's decade, and the 19th century.

Events

Births

Alfred Hitchcock, film director (13th August, 1899)



Deaths

Charles Pillsbury, founder of the Pillsbury company (died 17th September, 1899, aged 56)
