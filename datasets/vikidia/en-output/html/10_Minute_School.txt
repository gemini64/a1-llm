Ten Minute School (10 Minute School) is an Internet-based educational organization founded by education entrepreneur Ayman Sadiq. Bangladeshi mobile operator Robi initially supported it. Their goal was to create a place where people could efficiently study if they wanted. This organization produces short lectures on YouTube, Facebook, and their website. This website or organization produces video class lectures in Bengali.
The Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of this organization is Ayman Sadiq.
