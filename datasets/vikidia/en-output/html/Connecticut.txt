Connecticut is a state in the northeastern United States. In 2015, it had 3,590,886 people. (*)
Hartford is the state capital, and Bridgeport is the largest city.
Connecticut is bordered by Massachusetts to the north, Rhode Island to the east, the Long Island Sound (part of the Atlantic Ocean) to the south, and New York to the west.
