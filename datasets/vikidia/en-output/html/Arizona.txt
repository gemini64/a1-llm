Arizona is a state in the southwestern United States. As of 2015, it had 6,828,065 people. (*)
Phoenix is the state capital and largest city. Tucson is the second largest.
Arizona is bordered by Utah to the north, Colorado to the northeast, New Mexico to the east, Mexico (states Sonora and Baja California) to the south, California to the west, and Nevada to the northwest.
