An elk
Elks are a type of deer.
They are slightly smaller than a horse.
The elk is a mammalian animal.
They have a bag under the neck that is called "bell".
It weighs around 500 kg.
They eat birch and willow leaves.
It has big horns.

Scientific denomination and classification
The scientific name of the elk is alces

Behaviour
The elk is a herbivourous animal.

Habitat
It lives in the northernmost areas of Eurasia and North American.

Evolution
Elks are mammals

Its presence in the fables and stories
The elk is almost never present in stories.
