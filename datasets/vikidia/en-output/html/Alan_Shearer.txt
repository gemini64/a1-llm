Alan Shearer CBE DL (born 13 August 1970) is an English football pundit and former professional player who played as striker. He has scored the most goals in the Premier League with 260 goals.
When he was younger he played for Wallsend Boys Club and then Southampton Under-21s from 1986–1988. When he was over 21, he played for Southampton from 1988–1992, for Blackburn Rovers from 1992–1996, and for Newcastle United from 1996–2006.
He also played for England U21 from 1990–1992, England B in 1992, and England from 1992–2000.
